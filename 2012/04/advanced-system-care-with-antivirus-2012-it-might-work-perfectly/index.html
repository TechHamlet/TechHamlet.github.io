Advanced System Care With Antivirus 2012 : It Might Work Perfectly!

Apr 21st, 2012 | Comments

Advanced System Care is our top system utility suit. If you want a clean healthy machine, ASC is the best choice. Recently, they have started to integrate a small malware scanning feature into it while developing a full feature anti malware as a different product. Now, they are going to integrate a full Anti Malware system into Advanced System Care! I think it will be more effective than their Malware Fighter PRO.

Several years ago, IObit released an anti malware app named Security 360. It become very effective and came neck to neck with existing malware scanners. But it failed because of a small error made by one of its users. The company then stopped developing it and recently, release IObit Malware Fighter which is a really good cloud base malware scanner. While Security 360 aimed to work as a second line of defence, Malware Fighter wanted to become a fully featured anti malware app. But now, Advanced System Care might steal its place.

The best thing about IObit is, they accept their mistakes and learn from them. They always listen to the user. Two times they tried to build their own anti malware engine and now they have a good experience. Their own anti malware engine is not new. It have several years of experience. But the new ASC 2012 will also have another anti malware engine…

IObit have partnered with Bitdefender and included their anti-virus engine in their upcoming ASC 2012. According to the latest test results, Bitdefender is the number one anti-virus engine with the highest detection rate and accuracy. IObit have made a very wise decision.

It’s not only a scanner. The new ASC 2012 will also give real-time protection to the user. But, if you already have an anti-virus in your machine, it won’t enable the real-time protection until you remove it. This is really good because two heads can conflict.

Earlier, several system utility suits tried to do the same thing but they didn’t work much. The reason is, people won’t trust a new anti-virus app. They need something which is really effective and have a good record. That’s why I said IObit is wise. They have added their own and the world’s number 1 anti-virus engine to their utility suit.Â I see this as a combination of the number 1 antivirus engine and the number 1 system utility suit!

Some people might still want to use their existing anti-virus software. Of course they might have better real-time protection features. ASC still have a lot to improve on that side. But what I’ll do is, keep it with the real-time protection turned off. Their Bitdefender powered scanner is really valuable.

I also hope that they’ll keep the normal Anti Virus free ASC as a separate product for those who need only system optimizations. Lets see… If you want to try it, you are free to do it but be careful. It’s still in beta stage and might be carrying a lot of bugs. If you dare to take the challenge, install it. Otherwise, wait till the stable software comes out. If you want to help IObit, go here (https://www.facebook.com/questions/370029889705985/) and tell them whether you like this new idea or not!
