Top Web Design Trends of 2012

Jan 27th, 2012 | Comments

Are you ready for the blasts of 2012? 2011 gave us much more, but in 2012, the young talented developers won’t stay quiet and the battles of web designing will emerge in a new way. The fight of web design, the greater and the smarter interfaces will come up! And this year, we will see more and more innovations in the field of web designing. Let’s have a look at some web designing trends for the year 2012:

Design That Responds!

As the name describes it all, make responsive designs. This is the most important trend that will be followed by most of the web designers out there. You know very well that the time is not far when the mobiles or rather smart phones will take over the laptops, so better web designs will emerge which will facilitate the navigation on different screen sizes. That’s the origin of the responsive web design trend. And this year, it’s going to much more challenging as there is a plethora of developers out there.  A number of websites have started practicing this trend and they will be the market leaders soon. This trend is indeed new.

Flash

Flash is going nowhere again this time, and it will survive. Bigger sites might shift from flash, but for entertainment sites, there is no other choice. They have to come back with a bang. This year, there will be a much bigger return to flash.

Animations

Web designs have always contained animations and will do now as well. Animations created with HTML5, jQuery and CSS3 have been appreciated by most of the expert web designers and this is a clear indication that the talented developers will again be supported this year for some new animations. Animations are supported by experts because of the less coding lines.

Photographical Background

2012 is going to be really artistic for the websites at least. There will emerge an amazing trend of putting up huge images as background. This trend was seen in many sites in 2011 and it’s going to continue in 2012 as well. With the faster internet, and the availability of image optimizing tools, this all will be possible.

With a dynamic picture placed at the home page showing a great view that will mesmerize you, won’t you check out the site even if you don’t want to?

**Static Navigation

**

This type of navigation was introduced a few years ago, and was appreciated. Even at the end of 2011, this was seen in many sites and was again appreciated by the web designers around the globe. So there’s a strong possibility that it will come around again this year.

Infographics

As the term itself indicates, infographics is a very interesting trend that has been around for some time. It’s like representing information in image form. A lot of innovative forms will emerge gradually.

QUALITY AT ITS BEST, PRICE AT ITS BEST

As the newer things come up in 2012, the developers run towards professionalism. There will be no compromise when it comes to quality and no compromise when it comes to prices too!
