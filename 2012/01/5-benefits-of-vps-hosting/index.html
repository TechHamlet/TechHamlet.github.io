5 Benefits of VPS Hosting

Jan 23rd, 2012 | Comments

The World Wide Web has become an extremely powerful tool used by millions of people all around the world. Regardless of the fact whether you are a business owner and want to build a website for your business, or an inspiring blogger thinking about becoming popular and having his or her blog read by thousands of people every day, you will need the same thing – website hosting. There are basically two types of website hosting, and these are dedicated and shared website hosting.

Dedicated, Shared or VPS Hosting

The best thing about VPS (Virtual Private Server) hosting is the fact that it comes right in the middle of dedicated and shared hosting, and provides you with the benefits of both of these types of website hosting. So, it is no wonder why so many small and medium sized business owners, as well as other website owners and bloggers are rapidly moving to VPS hosting.

Read full Review of VPS vs. Shared Hosting

5 Benefits of VPS Hosting

There are tons of benefits of VPS hosting, and here will be explained the top five of them:

  • Control panel interface gives you root access – As opposed to shared hosting, you will have access to making all sorts of adjustments to the system software with VPS hosting. Full control is something that was reserved only for dedicated hosting, but can now come with VPS hosting as well.

  • VPS hosting as a cost-efficient solution – When compared to dedicated hosting, VPS hosting comes at a much more affordable price, but offers many benefits of dedicated hosting. Therefore, if you would not like to choose shared hosting, and cannot afford dedicated hosting, then VPS hosting will definitely be the perfect solution.

  • Traffic patterns can be easily recognized and catered for by VPS hosting – Many people have the problem of their websites not being able to go through heavy traffic with shared hosting. With VPS hosting, the server will automatically free all available resources for your traffic peak times, thus managing heavy traffic without any hiccups.

  • VPS hosting allows your website to grow bigger – If you have a website that is likely to grow bigger as time passes by, you will definitely have a problem with shared hosting as you are sharing the resources with others. VPS, on the other hand, will prove to be far more flexible in this arena.

  • Hosting several websites – With VPS hosting, you will be able to host more than one website, and this is not available with shared hosting – at least not if you want all of these domains to run smoothly. So, VPS hosting will allow all of these separate websites to be hosted with no performance issues whatsoever.

So, Is VPS Hosting the Best Solution for You?

Well, it seems that the answer to this question is positive. When you take into consideration that you will get a lot of benefits of dedicated website hosting and pay far less money for it, then it is pretty safe to conclude that you will not find a better solution for hosting your website than VPS hosting.
