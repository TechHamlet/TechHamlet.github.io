Best Deals From UK’s Number One Mobile Comparison Site

Jan 18th, 2012 | Comments

With the improvement of the technology and the internet, the rate of scams has also raised up. In a world where your book knowledge will soon be outdated within several years, the gadgets are also changing rapidly to comfort the human needs. My previous article mentioned about the importance of mobile phone recycling and why you shouldn’t recycle it for free. Most of my friends still drop their used cell phones to a box near the store to be recycled. They just do it for free. Why giveaway a product you bought for money for free? It’s always better to giveaway products for free if they are for a good cause. Here, do you know that the company which is collecting your phone, doesn’t give your cell phone for FREE. They charge the recycling plant an amount for that which goes directly as a PROFIT for that company.

It’s not eco friendly just to throw away your used cell phone; instead, you can think of a way to “sell my phone”. The Li ion batteries in your cell phones might be extremely hazardous. So please make sure that you let your cell phone be recycled or used by someone else so that we can reduce the carbon footprint. By doing this, you shouldn’t get any discomfort too. So the best way is to sell your mobile for recycling at the highest possible rate right?

Ever thought “How To Sell My Phone?”. Well here ’s the solution. Sellmymobile is a top site for that. It’s a UK based site but is available to many parts of the world. All the transactions are done online which means- NO DELAY!!!

This site contains autonomous software which will compare the model of the mobile you have entered against the current market rate and give you the best deal. Also this gives an array of companies who are willing to buy your mobile so that you can choose the company you need to sell to.

How to trade in?

  • Obviously first what you have to do is key-in your cell phone model.

  • Then sellmymobile will list all the available deals for that specific model with the price and other details.

  • You can choose the deal you want. (Obviously with the highest price)

  • All you have to do is follow the instructions of that company and post the cell phone to them.
