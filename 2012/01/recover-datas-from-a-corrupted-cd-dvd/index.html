Recover Data's From a Corrupted CD / DVD

Jan 15th, 2012 | Comments

CD’s and DVD’s are nowadays a part of our life. Mostly people use these things to carry their data from one place to another. These things mostly dont last longer. I mean that they may get corrupted because of scratches in due course of time. Even today I got one of my most important CD corrupted. So I started to search over the internet to find out softwares which recovered data from your damaged discs… This software is named as EASEUS Data  Recovery Wizard. This is a premium software but the free version is also available which limits the amount of data that could be recovered to just 1GB which would be more than enough if you are using a CD. It not only allows you to recover data from just your CD or DVD but also from your Hard Disk and even from your USB Drive !

Pros :

  • Free Version Available

  • Recovers data from even Hard Disks and USB Drives

Cons :

  • Free Version allows to recover data upto 1GB

  • It slows down at some times

The cost of the pro version is just $70 which is really affordable for a software like this. And also the pro version allows you to recover unlimited data’s.

You can download the Free Version from here : http://www.easeus.com/datarecoverywizard/free-data-recovery-software.htm
