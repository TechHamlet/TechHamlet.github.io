Access Facebook Without Internet on Any Mobile [Only for Indians]

Jan 14th, 2012 | Comments

The people in this world are currently greatly addicted to this fact called as Facebook. People nowadays are not even able to survive without facebook. The major gadget which people use to access facebook is mobile phones. But they feel that it would be great to have facebook free of cost to any mobile phones. Recently Facebook launched “ Facebook for Mobile ” which was not completely free too. So people out there got frustrated with facebook. But now here is a solution for them. This is an official thing that was made by Facebook India. This thing works even on the oldest mobile ever made (Even on Nokia 1100).

Facebook in combination with Fonetwish has launched this service which can be accessed by any Indian mobile which runs on one of the following carriers :

  • Aircel

  • Airtel

  • Tata Docomo

  • Idea

What you need to get this service is to just dial *325# to access this service. I have got a Nokia 5310 Xpress music mobile. I was not able to access this feature just because my mobile does not allow me to enter alphabetical letters when I reply to any service command. But this service works fine on other mobile phones which I tried out.

Nothing in this world is free. I gets from you a cost of just Rs.1 per day for unlimited data usage which I hope is really resonable. Here are some of the screenshots to show you guys out on how this service works.
