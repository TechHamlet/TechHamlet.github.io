Customizing Your Facebook Fan Page

Jan 19th, 2012 | Comments

Facebook is growing at a tremendous rate and most of the bloggers out there use Facebook to publicize their blog and make it more popular. Bloggers mainly aim at getting more likes to their fan page so that they could get more traffic to their blog. But without implementing the correct methods it is really impossible for people to get more fans. The most important thing that you need to do is to customize your facebook page to make it more attractive so that people would surely check out your page. So here are some of the optimization tips that would help you to increase the appearance of your facebook fan page. Lets get started.

Create a Landing page :

So at first what is a landing page ? It is the page which appears when an user who has not liked your page visits the page. Creating a landing page is not much difficult as you guys think it is. There are lots and lots of applications that are available to make your landing page ready in seconds.

I highly recommend this application for you guys to use. It allows you to add a landing page to your facebook page. But you need to add the codes to it and tell them what you need to add to the landing page. In this world you cant survive without coding.

If you dont wanna code this item then you can also use this application to add a welcome tab using a WYSIWYG editor/

Adding Extra Tabs To your Page :

This is a highly recommended trick that you need to follow. Add  more and more tabs to your facebook page containing more information that the users require. And even in this place you need to add codes to show what you need to appear in those tabs.

Adding tabs is also so simple. Here is a video tutorial on how to add such tabs to your facebook page.

add this video pubudu : http://www.youtube.com/watch?feature=player_embedded&v=LOclRPFiiAo

Adding a Contact Form :

This would make the users easier if they wanted to contact you. This would also increase your privacy as you can hide your Facebook profile from the page and also allow the users to contact you in just a click. You can add it by your own codes but it would be easier for you to add the contact form by using the applications which are available. It would also help in you increasing the appearance of your contact form.

Here is the most recommended application which I used to design the contact form for my page.

Add a Group Chat to your page :

This would help the users of your page to come in close contact with you and the other users of your page. This does not work similar to that of a Facebook group chat but in a different location and with a different appearance.

You can add it to your facebook page using this application.

Add RSS Feed To Your Fanpage :

Adding the RSS feed to your blog would make your blog more lively and make the users easier to take a peek at your posts on your blog. You need to code to add it to your facebook page. But if you are using feed burner, they are providing codes for you to add it to your facebook page. So if you use feedburner then navigate to Publicize > Buzz Boost and get your codes.

An alternative To All The Above :
