Rumored Features in Apple iPhone’s Next Generation

Aug 4th, 2012 | Comments

Image Source : WindKoh

Apple iPhone has remained unbeaten in the market for several years and the next generation iPhone 5 is expected to get released soon with some remarkable features. Some of the rumored features are bigger screen of 4-inches, credit-card like capacities including iWallet, slimmer metallic design, 4G LTE and bunch of other cool stuff. All the iPhone users are eagerly waiting for the next generation iPhone 5 which is expected to be released soon with some exciting features.

1. Improved Metallic Finish:

The next generation iPhone might be made of new “Liquid metal” which is combination of copper, nickel, zirconium, Titanium and other metals. This amorphous alloy is malleable similar to plastic and can be used for manufacturing very tiny parts. It has very high resistance to scratches and is of high-strength but the phone will be of very light weight. So, the next generation iPhone will be more elegant in design with sturdy body which cannot be broken easily unlike the previous models.

2. Bigger and Brighter Display:

One of the main drawbacks of previous model iPhones is that screen size is comparatively smaller than other Smart phones from Samsung or HTC. So, Apple has taken this into account and next generation iPhone will have a bigger screen of 4.6 inches and with better resolution and clarity than existing iPhones. The retina display is one of the important features from iPhone which boasts extreme clarity and higher pixel density or pixels per inch (ppi). So, the new iPhone will have better screen resolution and bigger display which is a double advantage for users. Also, Apple is expected to alter the technology behind its touch screen by using in-cell touch panels instead of the traditional LCD display present in previous models. The integration of touch sensors to LCD screen has various benefits like improving the sensitiveness of the screen, reducing the overall screen thickness, enhancing image quality and also reducing overall power consumption.

3. 4G LTE for faster data Transfers and Improved Battery:

Apple has already launched high-speed network technology with its iPad2 which was considered was practice trial for iPhones. The next generation iPhones will have 4G LTE support for faster data transfers and network connectivity. The main obstacle for introducing 4G technology was that it would consume more battery power and hence previous versions of iPhone did not have it. Now, with introduction of new design, the thickness would get reduced to some extent and hence battery will also be bigger to accommodate 4G technology.

4. iWallet Feature:

Apple is always known for innovations and patents and “iWallet” is yet another technology which got patented by Apple. It’s an application which will be installed in next generation iPhones and will assist users to manage their finances and bank accounts effectively. It makes use of Near Field Communication (NFC) technology for making credit card transactions while holding your device immediate to payment console.

5. Newer Connections:

The next-generation iPhones are expected to replace the 30-pin connector present in earlier models with a new 19-pin connector. Also, it will have support for HDMI which is absent in previous models and was considered as one of the major disadvantages of iPhone. Also the headphone jack which is usually on top is expected to be changed to bottom of the phone.

6. Newer Operating system:

The iPhone 5 will introduce the newer version of trademark iOS 6 operating system from Apple. The new operating system is expected to be much faster and secure than previous versions. It will also be packed with number of cool applications like the earlier versions.

7. Quad-Core Processor:

Another rumor is that Phone 5 will come packed with a faster quad-core processor for superior performance. The RAM is also expected to be increased as 2 GB to ensure smooth and hassle-free performance.

These are some cool features which are expected to be present in the next generation iPhone 5 which is likely to get released soon.
