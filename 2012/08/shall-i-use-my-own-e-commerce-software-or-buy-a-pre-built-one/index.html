Shall I Use My Own E-commerce Software or Buy a Pre-Built One?

Aug 7th, 2012 | Comments

Image Source : Mark Nockleby

Most of you already know about popular blogging platforms like Wordpress and Blogger. Just like them, if you need to sell your products online, you need to get help from an e-commerce software! You can build your own software or buy one. Like blogging platforms, ecommerce software doesn’t come cheap. So, before taking any decisions, here are some points to consider…

If you know the basics of web designing, building a small website is easy. If you dive further and study web programing like PHP and JavaScript, making a Blogging Platform isn’t as hard as you think. But since we have top-notch blogging platforms for free, we don’t care much about building our own. But e-commerce software are not cheap. But they are also not easy to build! Why is that?

There are many challenges you have to face in this industry. The main challenge you face is security! In a blog, a visitor doesn’t have to give any of his details to us. If he wants to comment, he use only the name and the email. He can also use Twitter and Facebook logins. That doesn’t give us access to their accounts. But when talking about shopping, we deal with their most sensitive data! The credit card numbers and bank details! Even if you don’t plan to use those data to drain their accounts, you have to think about hackers. You have to spend a lot of time patching up flaws in your antivirus software. It should be completely secure  or else your users are at a risk!

Even if you build a good software, if you choose a crappy host, it can greatly damage the security of your system. You need to make sure the host is complete safe or buy your own VPS or a Dedicated Server and set things up by your self. It’s not easy. Not easy at all!

Not only security, you have to think about payment gateways. How can I accept money from the user? In a normal e-commerce website, after you hit pay, everything happens magically. Its fast and simple. But it’s not so simple from the inside. The site need to find the origin of the credit card, its bank and then go to the bank to verify the details. Then it have authorize the payment. You need to be able to contact every bank in the world! Check this Wikipedia article about Payment Gateways and you’ll understand how complex it is.

Not only that, there are many areas you need to consider. Presentation, how to order the items, how to market them… It’s not only one unit. A collection of software components including payment gateways, the web interface, shopping cart, etc..

There are many things we ignore when we create a blog. It’s not strict. But when it comes to an e-commerce application, things are really strict. You need to make sure that your software is really stable and secure. That’s real hard work! So, are you ready to accept the challenge?

Now, you have two options…

  • Spend some money and buy a good e-commerce software built by experts and tested by thousands of developers and normal shoppers.

  • Or, learn web development really hard. Learn something like Ruby on Rails and build your own app which will be unique and if you are good, you can make it more secure and more reliable than any other paid app out their! Its your choice.

So, what will you pick?
