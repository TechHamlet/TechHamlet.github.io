[The Sunday Documentary] : Inside Google by National Geographic

Nov 11th, 2012 | Comments

What do you know about Google? Do you know its mission? What they want? If not, you better learn it before making another single search on Google. You must know how it became a big tech giant and what is it’s goal!

According to Google, their primary goal is to “organize all the world’s information and make it universally accessible and useful”! They think it will take nearly 300 years! A fascinating goal but a bit scary… The one who have all the world’s information can become the president of the whole world. They might be able to control everything. But is it possible? Lets not think that far… 300 years mean we are already dead when it happens!

Like most of the other big companies Google also started in a garage. They always wanted to make things done in the cheapest possible way. That’s one of the reasons which made them successful. But their company is not simple. They acquired many startups in-order to grow into more areas in technology. They want all the data…

Watch this week’s documentary and learn how Google grew into a world super power. Go inside Google’s headquarters and see how they work. How they think and what they want. Watch it before you make another single search.
