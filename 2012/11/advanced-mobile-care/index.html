Advanced Mobile Care : Your Most Trusted Utility Suit Goes Mobile!

Nov 29th, 2012 | Comments

Remember Advanced System Care? The world’s number one system utility suit? The guy which can protect and boost your PC performance instantly? Well, that guy is now coming to Android!

Several months ago, IObit came up with an idea of an Android app. Many people liked the idea because we don’t have a very good system utility tool in Android that we can trust. The Play Store is full of rouge and malware apps. So, we can easily get lost in there. But, we already know IObit. They have already produced the world’s best system utility suit for computers. So, why can’t they port it to mobile devices? They have done it!

Advanced Mobile Care

The new stable Advanced Mobile Care is now available for everyone through the Play Store. It have inherited its look and feel from its big brother the Advanced System Care. First you’ll be greeted by a step by step introduction to the new app. Then, you’ll get a big SCAN button just like in ASC. Press it and the app will scan your system for Malware, Running Tasks, Cache and Junk Files. Unlike in Windows, Android doesn’t have much dirt because it’s based on Linux. But still, it’s a good idea to keep the system clean.

Scan Results

The Advanced Mobile Care app is not just a simple system utility suit. It also have its own mawalre scanner to protect you against viruses, spyware, Trojan horses, worms and suspicious apps. It also have a real-time scanner!

IObit’s revolutionary Game Booster helps to boost the Games even in old PCs. A similar feature is also available in their mobile app. It automatically collect information about all the games you have installed on your mobile device and make sure they run without any lags and other disturbances.

Another very important feature is the Battery Saver! Smartphones and Tablets have a very bad habit of draining the battery life quickly. For a very wide-spread problem, IObit have an answer… The Battery Saver in Advanced Mobile Care is very simple. It have some predefined rules that you can apply. For example, the super saver mode will turn off AutoSync, WiFi, Mobile Data, Set the Brightness to Dim, Reduce the sleep time, Mute the sounds and turn off the Bluetooth. You can select the scheme you want or you can customize them according to your needs.

Battery Saver

For people who think more about the system privacy, you can use this app to lock your photos, videos and other files with a password! There are some apps that access these data and some rogue apps send these data to their own servers. So, this is a very useful feature.

Apart from those features, there are some more like the Task Killer and the App Manager. You have to download and explore this app to uncover all these features. So, what do you think? Did it really boost your device?
