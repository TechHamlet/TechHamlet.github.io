Taskk : The Next Generation to Do List Which Does the Planning for You

Nov 16th, 2012 | Comments

Taskk

There are so many To Do list apps. Many popular apps like Any.Do and Remember The Milk rules the market. But, they are just simple lists. The To Do list that we are going to review today cannot be compared with these guys. It’s not just a simple To Do list. It’s a full featured productivity booster! A next generation To Do App!

Why did I call it the next generation To Do app? Most of the apps let you create a list of tasks, remind you about due tasks and let you cut them off as you finish each of them. Taskk have this basic functionality. But it goes beyond it…

First, you need to create lists (or projects). Then you can arrange them according to the priority. Next, you have to add tasks to those lists. When adding, you have to give an estimated time frame to complete it. If you want, you can also set it to repeat every day, week, month, or year until a certain day.

While you are working on these lists, Taskk will let you track the progress of the project… How many hours left to complete the list…  How many tasks have I completed and how many tasks left to do… What is the deadline… etc. You can also share the list with a third-party member (with some one like your supervisor or the customer) so he can track your progress.

The biggest feature of Taskk…

…is the Planner. When can I complete the tasks? How can I find time for it? How can I plan my day? These are big questions which cannot be answered by normal To do apps. But Taskk have the answers. After you prepare the lists and the tasks, set the time needed to complete them and the priorities, you can head over to the Planner. The Planner shows the 7 days of the week divided into weeks. All you got to do is, set the number of hours you have to work on these lists on each day. For example, on Monday, I have 2 hours and on Tuesday I can work for 6 hours… After you set that, Taskk will automatically assign tasks to your day according to their time and the priority. A To Do list that can help you to plan your whole week!

That’s not all… There are many more features inside the app. You just have to sign in and explore. For most of you, the Free account will be enough. If you need some extreme planning, you might want to go for a paid account.

According to the developers, the site is fully responsive so you can access it through a mobile device. But, still there’s no Android or iOS app. Taskk developers said they will add it soon and we are waiting for it!

So, what do you think? Isn’t this what you wanted? Isn’t it the productivity booster I told you about? Try it and tell us what you think about Taskk!
