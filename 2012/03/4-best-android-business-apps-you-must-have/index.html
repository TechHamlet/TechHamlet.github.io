4 Best Android Business Apps You Must Have

Mar 5th, 2012 | Comments

Whenever you purchase a new phone, you just need to check if the software that you have got accustomed to, will work on the new phone or not. Whenever you get a handset that matches the OS of old one, probably it may not be a big issue. But moving from Symbian or Mac to Android may create big problems for those who aren’t accustomed to the new interface. There are over 3 million android apps and we are going to list out some of the must have Android apps for business.

There is a wide range of valuable software available in the Android market that helps you to switch from one phone to another. Finding those apps isn’t too hard, but once again you’d need a whole lot of time to figure out the good apps in the market. Here are few suggestions from our side that’ll help you in making the most out of your new phone; 4 best Android business apps that any smart phone user ought to have.

Best Free Android Apps

-Image Source - Flickr

#1. Google Voice

Android phones are very much benefited from Google Voice app. The profound OS integration allows unlimited and free text messaging with the help of standard mobile data connection. Irrespective of your voice, carrier, and data plan, applications coveted voice mail is featured with other applications like archiving and filing. Sprint users can take advantage of standard mobile number as their Google Voice number, thanks to the latest agreement between Sprint and Google.

#2. Google Maps with Navigation

Although Google Maps is a standard app from Google, you cannot use it with every phone. If you’ve a compatible Android smartphone, this app can of great aid while traveling and your phone can act like a wireless carrier. Installing and using this app on your Android phone is pretty simple, and it is a free app too.

It is one of the best navigation apps available with data connection, and the map delivery covers all the latest maps for the geographic areas.

#3. Launcher Pro

Stock program launcher from Android is fairly reasonable and has satisfactory features. But, you can see many other options at cheaper prices… With the new Launcher Pro app, you will be provided the access to standard Android application with scrollable docks. Launcher Pro makes use of less memory when compared to the HTC’s UI widget.

#4. AK Notepad

AK Notepad is another handy android app that can be used to make quick notes on the go. Using this app is pretty simple, and it’s a powerful app that has a reminder feature that alerts you about all the important meetings, and doesn’t let you lose track of any important appointments.

You can also export notes to dedicated apps like Evernote via e-mail or SMS; you can also change the overall appearance of the window and set up unique LED notifications for alerts. There are many other applications that helps us to backup android contacts or to backup android messages and other files.

Making of these 4 of the best Android business apps, you’ll certainly get extracting tremendous benefits from your latest Android smartphone!
