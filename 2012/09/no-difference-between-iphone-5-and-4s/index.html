Is There Any Major Difference Between iPhone 5 and iPhone 4s? No?

Sep 13th, 2012 | Comments

What is the biggest difference you see between iPhone 5 and the iPhone 4s? Of-course you’ll see a difference in the above images because the color difference. But if they are both the same color, I don’t think you can tell the difference without putting them together.

Ok, the new iPhone is a bit longer and have a 4 inch screen! Big deal! We have already seen even bigger screens in the latest Android and Windows Phone devices. For example, the Galaxy S III have a 4.8 inch display with 720 x 1280 resolution!

Did you checked the recent video I posted in our forum : [Video] Here’s the new iPhone 5! What do you think? (They just gave an iPhone 4s but people took the Bait!). According to that video, you can easily trick some one and make him think he is using the new iPhone 5 by just giving the old 4s. They won’t see the difference. Because? Apple didn’t changed much since the first iPhone.

Same goes for the iOS. Those icons, that same look… Every day when speaking about the iPhone, I see the same thing. I can’t understand why it’s so special. See Android for an example… Can you tell me the difference between the first version and the latest version 4.1? There’s a big difference. With each new version Google does something big. Something that we can feel. But is there any big difference in iOS? Most of the features they introduced in this new device, we have already seen them in existing smart phones.

Apple did change something… Something that they shouldn’t have? What do you think about the new connector of iPhone 5? Many complain that they have to buy brand new accessories for the new device. They can’t use the old ones because the connector is different. It might be faster but do you think its worth it? I’m not going to comment in here…

This explains why Apple keeps patenting every single curve of their device and every single icon of their OS… They want to keep them as it is for a very long time!

Apple say that they introduced multitasking. But it have its limitations. Not even close to the true multi tasking functionalities we have in Android. If you need to get the maximum out of their new A6 processor, they need to have true multitasking. Otherwise its useless!

Don’t you think I’m telling the truth? Many people have already sold their old iPhones to buy the new one! Getting ready to experience some small changes with iPhone 5? Why not buy a cheaper Android phone and enjoy better features? Don’t be angry with me… I’ll tell you why… I don’t know whether its valid for the whole world, but in our country, some teens think that having an iPhone will make them look rich! They use it just for the show! What we can do is, just laugh and use our heads to pick better smart phones which can do more things better than the iPhone! What do you think?
