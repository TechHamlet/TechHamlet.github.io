OLX : Place Your Ads for Free and Meet the Buyers!

Jul 2nd, 2012 | Comments

If you need to sell anything without paying any fees to third parties, where will you go? Have no idea? Try OLX.in. Its simple, effective, popular, and also completely FREE!

If a normal person want to sell one of his possessions, he have to place an ad in the newspaper or go to a site and put the ad. Normally, we have to spend some money to place these ads. So, less profit! But OLX.in is a well established site which will allow you to place your classified ads for free.

Even if its free, you don’t wont to place your ad on a site which have a very less amount of visitors right? Whats the point of placing an ad if you can’t get good buyers? Don’t worry, OLX is formed in 2006 and currently, they have expanded their services to many countries around the world. There are separate sections for each country. Not only countries, they even have sections for separate cities! They also run frequent TV commercials (as seen below) to attract more people to the site. You place the ad and they’ll get the buyers!

OLX is not only for selling goods, you can also advertise your services. I have seen many people like web designers, movers, writers , translators and even drivers offering their services through OLX.

Unlike the others, OLX.in is well designed and well-organized. It’s really easy to use. If you need to go beyond free ads, you can also pay a little fee to OLX and move your ad to the featured section. You can place these featured ads in OLX home page, search results and even on the top of the listings.

If you wont to go mobile with OLX, you’ll be glad to know that they offer separate apps for Android, iPhone, iPod Touch, Nokia and Windows Phones.

Free, well organized, feature rich, and easy to use! What else do you need? Just head over to OLX.in and SELL IT!
