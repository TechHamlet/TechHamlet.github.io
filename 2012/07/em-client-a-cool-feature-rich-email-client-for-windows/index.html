eM Client : A Cool Feature Rich Email Client for Windows

Jul 13th, 2012 | Comments

Recently, Mozilla announced that they are going to stop adding new features to their famous email client Thunderbird. They’ll gradually stop developing it. So, its time to find alternatives!

eM Client is the first one I tried. It’s free for home use and have a very rich feature list. One of the first features I noticed is the nice email sorting. In the inbox, eM client sort all our emails into the first few days of the week (Mail received Today, Yesterday, Monday…) and then sort into weeks. It’s really nice and make things easy for us. The interface have a slight polished look but it’s too cluttered. Too much of everything might be a problem… Too many features have cluttered the UI. I want my software to be really simple and easy to use.

When you select an email, you can view most of the details of it in the right panel and it’ll also give a list of past emails sent by the same person. In that same panel, you can even chat with your friends. Not only chat in your email like Gtalk and Yahoo Chat, you can even add Facebook and XMPP / Jabber chat accounts.

Not only email, it have Calendar, Tasks, Skype integration, and even more features can be added through Widgets. A really good choice for those who want a lot of features. But for those who wont clean and simple interfaces, you might have to wait. I also faced another problem with eM client… Its only available for Windows! So, what do you think? Will you go for it?
