Head to Head: Magento vs. Prestashop, Which Is the Better Platform

Dec 12th, 2012 | Comments

Image Source : Fosforix

If you are struggling to make a decision on what platform to choose then here is a comparison between Magento eCommerce and Prestashop.

Two different platforms- both serving same purpose, but which one is better than the other. On one side you have a platform, one the best shopping cart platforms, and on the other hand you have a platform that is a new but is carving its niche in the market. Both these platforms are highly recommended by their users. Both of them are user-friendly and have already proved their worth but there has always been a debate of which one is better.

Let’s first take a look at each of them individually and then we will compare them to see who emerges as the winner,

Magento: - Magento has been here for quite some time and has powered many websites. It is loaded with features, very flexible and easy to use. Also a great platform for marketing, SEO and other activities because this platform is SEO friendly. Magento came over a decade back and since then has had some advanced versions of it and they have never failed to find favor with their users. It also has a great support team so you will not have any problems and would be able to get a solution to pretty much anything.

Prestashop: - Being opensouce we can say that it can never disappoint. It came about 5 years back and since then it has never looked back. PrestaShop makes use of the Smarty template engine for PHP. It is also available in over 40 languages but only English and French have the complete support system available. Though it does not have a tech support team, it has a forum from where you can get your problems solved by talking to other developers.

Now that we have known them, let’s jump into the comparison, Though they are great in their own rights but comparisons like this brings out some points that will help us choose according to our needs.

Magento vs. Prestashop

  • Prestahop when compared to Magento does not have as many features. It is easy to install Prestashop but it definitely gets a beating from Magento when it comes to features.

  • The system requirements that one needs to have is less with Prestashop compared to Magento but Magento is free from many of the compatibility issues that you can face when you use Prestashop.

  • If you compare the search capabilities then Prestashop is a clear winner in that case.

  • As I said earlier than Magento is loaded features and so you get a lot of themes and extensions compared to that of Prestashop.

  • Magento is designed for anything from small to huge eCommerce websites while Prestashop is designed for smaller companies who want to sell online.

  • While Magento has a strong tech support team, all Prestashop has is a forum where you might not get instant support for your problems. So in that case Magento has a better stand.

  • Magento is known for being Search Engine friendly which is not the case with the other.

  • If we compare both on the basis of functionalities it is the wish list, customer groups, product comparison, RSS feed and TAG that are the highlights in Magento. Apart from that you can also manage multiple e-stores through a single admin. On the other hand, Prestashop also has its own unique features like statistics, shipping tracking and membership.

  • The membership of Magento is much larger than that of Prestashop which is because it has been there for over a decade but has always been successful. Prestashop is expected to rise to that level in the near future.

After comparing both of them what I can say is that Magento can be a better platform when we consider various aspects while Prestashop is not bad choice for businesses who are starting out and do not have too much to offer to their audience. So you can choose what you want based on the size of your requirement.
