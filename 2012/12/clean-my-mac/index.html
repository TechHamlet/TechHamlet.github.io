Use Clean My Mac to Keep Your System Healthy and Junk Free

Dec 10th, 2012 | Comments

Clean My Mac

Unlike in Windows, Linux and Mac doesn’t have much junk to slow down the machine. They don’t have messy registry keys (they have similar implementations but they are far more cleaner and well-managed than on Windows), they don’t need defragmentations, the OS doesn’t need much tweaks because they are already tweaked… But that doesn’t mean they are completely junk free!

Mac have unnecessary cache files, logs, trash, left overs from uninstalled apps and more system junk files. To get rid of these garbage, we can use one little app named Clean My Mac…

When you first open the app, you’ll notice that it have a very clean easy to use interface. Well designed and self-explanatory. There are 6 main categories in which it can scan your system… Cache files, Log files, Unused language files, Universal binaries which won’t be used in your system, Various system junk files and Leftovers from removed apps, widgets and broken preference panes…

Not only that… The app can also help you to list and remove unwanted widgets that you haven’t used for a long time… When you uninstall apps from any operating system, some will leave behind junk. For Windows, there are special programs like the Revo Uninstaller to get rid of these stuff. For Linux, we can completely purge the packages. For Mac you can use Clean My Mac’s uninstaller which will scan for left overs and remove the application completely. You just have to drag and drop the application you want to remove.

Uninstall

The quick erase feature will help you to remove anything without moving to trash. It can also remove apps which might be write protected. In here, you need to be careful not to delete any important files! Just like most of the other utility systems, this eraser also have a secure erase feature which will remove sensitive date so that they cannot be easily recovered!

A handy little tool to speed-up your Mac by getting rid of the junk! What do you think? Try it today and let us know what you think about Clean My Mac.
