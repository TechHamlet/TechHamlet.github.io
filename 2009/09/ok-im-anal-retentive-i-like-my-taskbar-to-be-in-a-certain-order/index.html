OK, I'm Anal-retentive, I LIKE My Taskbar to Be in a Certain Order

Sep 17th, 2009 | Comments

Maybe this isn’t a big deal to you, but for me it is.  I always like having outlook as the first thing on my taskbar (furthest on the left).  The problem is if I restart outlook, it loses its treasured position on the taskbar (since the order is the starting order of the programs).

What I want to see:

What I see after restarting Outlook:

Short of shutting down everything and restarting the programs, how can I get the order on the taskbar to be what I want?

I found a free program called Taskbar Shuffle that does the trick.  It runs in XP and Vista.

Taskbar Shuffle

Once you install the program, all you need to do is drag the program windows in the taskbar to the order that you want them to be.
