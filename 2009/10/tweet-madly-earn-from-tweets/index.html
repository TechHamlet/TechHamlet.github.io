Do You Tweet Madly? Want to Earn From Your Tweets?

Oct 14th, 2009 | Comments

Have you ever thought about earning from your tweets? Well…. now twitter has become one of the must use services for most of us! Most of us love to tweet, some spends most of their time in twitter! But didn’t you know that you can earn from your tweets? Actually there are many ways which you can earn from twitter……..

One day when I was searching for new things on the web, I found one site which you can use to earn from twitter! Then I searched more and found two more…. currently Im using one of them, and earning a certain amount of money! So are ready to hit the jackpot? Here we go then….

1) Be a Magpie

The days where large publishing houses were the only ones which reached the large audiences are long gone. With Twitter, we are at the dawn of a new era, where everyone can be a publisher, where everyone can be heard and everyone is listened to.

At Magpie, we know that being a publisher means serious work. You guys entertain your followers, break the news and even sometimes make them laugh. We think that you deserve to be rewarded for that.

Magpie takes social media to the next level by allowing you to start earning from your tweets – just like the big publishing houses did back when they very still the cool kids.

2) Twivert

Twivert provides “value ads” which you can share with your friends through twitter. When someone clicks on these ads you get paid, thats it.

  • Twivert offers a monetizing opportunity for quality twitterers.

  • Auto-Tweet enables you to sit back and relax, we select appropriate CPC ads for you and tweet them on your behalf.

  • On the other hand you may choose to disable auto-tweeting and review the ads before publishing them.

  • Control the number of tweets being published.

  • Specify the timings when you want the tweets to be published.

  • Once you earn $30 you can request a payout!

3) Ad Cause

AdCause matches advertisers with publishers (twitter users). Publishers will get paid show ads in their twitter feed. The more influence you have on twitter, the more money you will make.

To join and use adCause you will need the following:

  • Twitter Username and Password (this is how we interface to twitter to display ads)

  • Information like: Your name, phone number, address, email address

  • If you live in the US: Your SSN or TAX ID Number (You don’t want the tax man to come a knocking do ya?)

Use them wisely! I don’t think its good to use more than one of these services! (Too many beans will spoil the soup!) So enjoy your new hobby!
