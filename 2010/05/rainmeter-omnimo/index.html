Desktop Customizations : Turn Your PC Into a Windows Phone 7

May 18th, 2010 | Comments

Got board of the default windows desktop that greets you everyday? Well, to cure this sickness, there are lots of themes that you can use to change the look of your desktop. But most of them have the same structure! What if we can use a theme with a completely different look? A theme which will turn your PC into a mobile phone? Sound interesting isn’t it?

Most of you might have heard of RainMeter which is a popular tool that we use to theme our windows desktops. This tool have lots of cool themes which we can use to completely change the look of our desktop. Today we are going to talk about one of those themes which will turn your desktop into a Windows Phone 7!

A brief description of the theme written by the developers :

Windows Phone 7 inspired complete rainmeter desktop suite, designed to be functional, and easily customizable as well as configurable. This suite will turn your desktop into a productive and nice looking space which you can customize every day. The interactive panels provide specific information so you wont have anything unnecessary on your desktop.

What do you need at first?

  • Rainmeter 1.2 r380 or higher (Remember we need Reainmeter 1.2r380 which is currently a beta or higher which comes with Rainstaller)

  • Omnimo 2 for Rainmeter which can be downloaded from this link

How to install this theme?

  • First install Rainmeter which we downloaded in the above steps.

  • After that, run Rainmeter at least for once and close it. To close it, right click on the Rainmeter icon on the task-bar and click “Exit”

  • Now, extract the contents of the “Omnimo 2 for Rainmeter” that you have downloaded

  • Go to Main Menu > Programs > Rainmeter > Rainstaller

  • After that, select “Omnimo.rmskin” which is located within the extracted files

  • Now, select “Express Install” as shown below :

  • Finally, run “Rainmeter” and you’ll be greeted by your new Omnimo desktop!

Now its time to enjoy your new desktop. Above is the screenshot of my desktop which have Omnimo installed in it! What do you think? Do you like the new theme? Please feel free to share your ideas with us!
