A More Safer Web With Norton DNS!

Jun 13th, 2010 | Comments

Normally when you enter a web address in your address bar, anything could come up. Mal-ware, spy-ware, viruses, trojans and many more harmful stuff can infect your system with infected web sites. When you type a web address, your system goes to a DNS server to get the IP of that web site. A normal DNS server will give you anything without even checking whether its malware! But what if the DNS server provides only safe sites?

I think some of you might remember that earlier we talked about custom DNS services : Surf the Web Faster and Safer With Custom DNS. In that post, we talked about Google DNS and Open DNS. These DNS services will provide you a more faster, and a safer web experience. These services will block dangerous websites from even coming closer to your system.

Recently, Norton have also decided to jump into the custom DNS field! Just like Google and Open DNS, now Norton will also provide Norton DNS which is said to provide a much safer web experience. I don’t know about the speed, but when it comes to safety… I think Norton can do a better job than the others because already Norton is an expert in computer security.

I also tested Norton DNS, but at first, it even started to block most of the ads (specially adsense ads) in web sites. For some people, it might be a great news. But for webmasters like us… its simply not good! But when I test it today, it didn’t block my ads like before. This shows us one thing… Norton might be a security expert, but they might want to learn more in the DNS field.

Even at first, people started to say that Google DNS is slower than your normal DNS servers. But what I think is, Norton and Google need to learn more in this field. For me, the real expert is Open DNS!

What do you think? Do you agree with me? Or do you have a different point?
