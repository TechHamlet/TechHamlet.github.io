Microsoft Office 2010 Hits the Market Today!

Jun 15th, 2010 | Comments

Since the first beta release last year, most of us waited for a long time, counting our fingers for this day to come. Now, finally its here! Today, the most awaited Microsoft Office 2010 have been finally released! So… what are you waiting for? Download a trial or buy Office 2010 and feel the power of its new powerful features which will surely make your lives a lot more easier!

On November 20, 2009, we posted our first post about Office 2010 when Microsoft released its first beta version : Want to Have a Bite of MS Office 2010?. Even from the first look, most of us knew that its going to be a great hit. Even Softpedia says that Office 2010 has gone bigger than Windows 7!

These days, the Internet is becoming very popular around the world and most of the software out there works with the help of the net. So, the latest version of Office have more features for you to work with the web. More tools to make everything more easier than ever. I think this version have more attractive features than most of the earlier versions that they have released.

Office is becoming very popular and Microsoft have also decided to offer a free version of MS Office online. I think most of you might have seen the article that we posted about Office Live Web Apps today : How To Bring The Office Web Apps to Your Desktop

Years ago, with the release of Windows Vista, Microsoft’s popularity started to die away. Vista got lots of criticism, and I think Microsoft went down very rapidly because of this problem. But, the recent release of Windows 7 was able to bring some hope to Microsoft. Windows 7 became so much popular and it was able to spread through the world like a virus. So, I think these new releases like Office Live Web Apps, Office 2010 and Live Wave 4 will make Microsoft rule the world again! But one thing…. we should not give them the complete control… or else they’ll try to conquer us like ………..!

Download Free Trial or Buy Office 2010
