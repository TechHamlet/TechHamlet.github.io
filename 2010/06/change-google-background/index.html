Lets Change the Background of Google!

Jun 7th, 2010 | Comments

Recently Google have done a lot of changes to their search engine. Specially they have done a lot of improvements to their interface. The new sleek interface surely became very popular among the people and many waited for a long time for these new changes to take effect. But recently, we found out a new trick that Google have added to its interface! Want to know what?

Recently, we found out that Google have been testing a new feature which will allow you to add a background image to Google’s home page. You can choose any image that they have already uploaded, or upload any image in your local hard drive!

How to use this new feature :

  • Currently this will only work on Google.com, so you need to go to Google.com if you were greeted by your local Google home page. To do this, you can click on Go to Google.com link located in the left hand side. (right under the search box).

  • Next click on “Change background image” link located at the bottom left corner of Google Home Page :

  • After that, if you haven’t logged into your google account, they’ll redirect you to the login page. Enter your user name and password and log in.

  • After logging in, if you didn’t see the background chooser in Google home page, click on the “Change background image” link again. Then you’ll see the image chooser as shown below :

  • Now, select your background image and click “Save”

I think Google knows how to impress a user! So, What do you think about this new feature?
