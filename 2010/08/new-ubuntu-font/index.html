HowTo Try Out the New Ubuntu Font in Your System?

Aug 4th, 2010 | Comments

Ubuntu was able to do a lot of contribution to the Linux world in the past few years. Recently, they did a lot of changes to the look of their famous OS like going to a completely different boot screen and a completely different theme to impress their users! But still its not enough for them.. Now they are trying to go for a completely different Font for their famous Linux system!

With the release of Ubuntu 10.04, they also released a new boot screen with a new Ubuntu logo. So, have you looked carefully at that logo? Not only that, even in the Uubuntu Website, they are using a logo like that with the same font. But unfortunately, that font is only limited to the Ubuntu logo and we were unable to see or use it elsewhere.

But now, that problem is going to be solved! The guys at Ubuntu are trying their best to build a completely new Font just like the one they have used in their logo. They have been developing this font on their labs for a long time but recently, they released it as a beta for us to test it. So.. don’t you want to test it out? Lets see how to do that..

  • First you’ll need a user account at Kubuntu Users. Go to Kubuntu Users and create an account or login from your existing launchpad account.

  • After your done, click on this link : http://launchpad.net/people/+me/+archivesubscriptions
