Things to Consider When Designing Client Websites

Aug 16th, 2010 | Comments

With more and more companies expanding there business online web design has become an important factor web designers are in high demand. If you are a web designer or aspiring to be one below are some things you should consider when you are building your clients website.

Using Flash and Images

Designing websites for a client could be a tricky thing, for one thing most of them want a fancy looking website full of flash animations which doesn’t necessarily mean a good website. Websites built mainly using flash tent to load very slowly thus leading to poor usability and they tend to look less favorably on search engines as well. On the other hand very plain looking website is also not good, you need to attract your visitors and grab your audience and images help in that regard. Finding that perfect balance is the trickiest thing of all. You can make your choice according to the type of the website as well. If its a website for a hotel then most of the time visitors will look for images because they want to see how the hotel looks like. So having only few images in the home page and a link to an image gallery is alright. But if its a website about mobile phones then a visitor might come there looking for a specific product but might be tempted to check out more products if he saw some cool looking product. You have to make that decision in compliance with your clients demand.

Optimizing for search engines

It is true that you are just building a website for your client and not looking to rank it in search engines but there are some basic on page optimization that you can do to help in that process. In the competitive world of web site design this could be a factor that can give you the edge. This could be a simple thing like having a proper meta tags, having a title with proper keywords etc. Keep in mind that words like SEO, Search engine optimized are buzzwords that potential customers are looking for. They don’t know what it is but they do know that it is good for there business and website. Potential deal breaker in this competitive business.

Its not the looks but the usability and usefulness

Although looks are important the most important aspect of a website is its usability. You can have the most fancy looking website but if the potential visitors can’t find the information they are looking for then it want be much you for them or your customer. So make sure increase the usability of the website you are building as well. It could be adding descriptive links for easy navigation, adding a search box to search your site, having a proper error page etc which are things that are not given prominence when it comes to web design in Sri Lanka.
