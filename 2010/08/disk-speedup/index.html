Lets Boost Your Disks With Glarysoft Disk SpeedUp

Aug 17th, 2010 | Comments

Disk SpeedUpFor now most of the TechHamlet readers know that there are several ways to tweak the performance of your Windows machine! Among those Tweaks, Registry Cleaners and Disk cleaners.. disk Defraggers also performance a very important role. A Disk Defrag tool will organize the files in your hard disk for optimum performance. So, today we are going to talk about a new addition to this mighty Defragger family!

Normally, the normal users will never see how their data were stored in their hard disks. They might see their data organized well into several folders, but the real arrangement can be a total mess! Parts of one program might be stored in several places.. so your hard disk have to spin more to get those data.

This is where the disk defraggers come into play! These guys will carefully organize your data in the hardisk. After they nicely pack your disks, you might get a great performance boost. So, what are you waiting for? Lets see what we got today..

As I said earlier, Glarysoft’s Disk SpeedUp is the newest addition to the Disk Degrag family. These system tuneup experts will offer this great price of software completely free of charge. But still its packed with a lot of interesting features that you should check :

  • It is a very light weight app which won’t take much of your resources

  • The very user-friendly interface will allow anyone to get the maximum use out of this software

  • The auto defragging feature will automatically make the software to wake up and do the defragging while your system is idle! Anyway… its a good idea to put your system into some use while your not using it!

  • This software will also allow you to schedule the defragment tasks to run on a previously set time. This little feature is absent in most of the free defrag tools out there!

  • They will also allow you to defrag any individual file or folder that you select. Which is also absent in other free defrag tools

A very good set of useful features which we can’t refuse! Don’t you agree? So, what are you waiting for? Just try it out and tell me what you think about it!
