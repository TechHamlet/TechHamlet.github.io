Updated : Video : See Whats the Fastest Browser by Yourself!

Sep 27th, 2010 | Comments

You might have already chosen the perfect web browser for your day today browsing. The first thing you might have checked must be the speed of that browser… But wait.. Have you ever tried to run a speed test to pick the fastest browser or did you picked it because some one just told you that its the fastest? Today we are also going to show you whats the fastest browser… But we have solid proof!

Almost all the browsers claims that they are the fastest! But, who’s telling the truth? There should be only one winner! The browser manufacturers wants to promote their own products.. So, we cannot trust them in this situation! Some people might just show some graphs and reports and then tell us that the a certain browser is the fastest.. But we need solid proof right? Something which can show us clearly what is the fastest browser in the planet!

To solve this problem, we decided to test the top 5 browsers, record the tests, and put it in a video which will clearly show you who’s the king! This is how we did it :

  • First we cleared all the cache and history from the browsers to make sure that the site is not loaded from the local cache.

  • Next, we opened each browser and used them to visit our site (http://www.techhamlet.com) and recorded everything with a screen capture software.

  • Finally, we combined all the clips to a single video and added timers (these timers might differ a little from the real world minutes and seconds, but all the timers are same.. So, the final results won’t change!) to measure the speed of each browser!

So, want to see the video? Here it is :

The Final Results :

First Place : Google Chrome 6

Second Place : Safari 5

Third Place : Opera 10.62

Fourth Place : Firefox 4 Beta

Last Place : Internet Explorer 9 Beta

To tell you the truth.. I had high hopes about the new IE 9 Beta, but it came last! My favorite Google Chrome, remains at the top and Safari have to do only a little more to beat Chrome!

This video is not from a single person! Its a product of our strong TechHamlet team! Thank you everyone who supported to make this a success! Special thanks goes to Sahan Serasinghe who edited the soundtrack!

So, What do you think? Did this video change your favorite browser? Or, have you already made the correct decision? Please feel free to share your ideas with us! Enjoy!

Note : Everyone, please note that we used a 512kbps ADSL connection to make these tests and we are not from US where they offer more bandwidth for lower rates ! So, the site will look like that its very slow.. But I assure you that our site doesn’t have such problems. (Pingdom Tools clearly proves that). Still I don’t think this will have an effect in the final results (Not the load times, the places (1st.. 2nd.. 3rd…..)). (Please tell me if im wrong!)

Due to this problem, a lot of people have simply blamed the design of our site, or have blamed that the tests were wrong! Some have even stated that our site is bloated!You can see these comments by looking at our comments section below. But, People at DownloadSquad.com gracefully showed us where we went wrong through Facebook. They are the only people who acted with some respect.. So, thank you very much DownloadSquad!
