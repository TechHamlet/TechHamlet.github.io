Introducing the New AVG 2011!

Sep 29th, 2010 | Comments

AVG is very popular for their famous free antivirus protection. Even the recent AV Tests proved that AVG is still capable of fighting against most of the viscous malware threats that comes to attack your systems.. That powerful AVG, Just got even more powerful! AVG have finally released their latest version, AVG 2011!

As I said earlier, when it came to free antivirus protection, AVG was one of the best choices that we had! People always trusted them to provide a good security for their systems for free. So, AVG Free is the most popular product that made them very popular.

Yesterday, AVG released a major new version for all of their products. The new AVG 2011 comes with a more improved web protection powered with their popular Link Scanner. In the latest version, the Link Scanner will also scan links in your social network profiles like Facebook and MySpace allowing you to click on them safely!

The installation process was modified to be more simpler by reducing it to five simple clicks. At first, you might think that AVG has become more lighter because the setup only weighs about 4 MB! But.. during the installation.. it downloads the rest of the packages which counts upto about 120 MB.

The new AVG will have a game mode feature which is becoming one of the essentials for an antivirus system. People who have used the latest Kaspersky 2011 might have seen their new desktop widget.. AVG also have a widget like that which enables you to easily access its features from your desktop.

According to AVG, apart from the looks, The new AVG 2011 which users their Smart Scan technology will be able to attack the malware trying to harm your PC more efficiently. Not only that, the new system needs lesser resources to operate. So, even your old PC is now safe!

This and many more other features have been introduced in the new AVG system. To tell you the truth, a normal machine always needs antivirus protection with a good firewall.. But AVG Free still lacks firewall protection. But that doesn’t mean you cannot use it.. You can still use AVG with the free Comodo firewall to get a very good protection for your system for free. So, what do you think?
