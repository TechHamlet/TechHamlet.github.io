A Quick Overview of the Year 2010 With Google Search

Dec 11th, 2010 | Comments

Every day people use Google to search for their interests! Google Zeitgeist “the spirit of the times” will collect data from millions of these search queries and prepare a report at the end of every year which will give us a clear overview of the whole year which we are going to leave behind! This report will tell us what are the trending topics which made that year special!

Think that there’s something special going on… or something disastrous… The first thing most of us does is, go to the web and search to learn more about it.. Think that there’s something or someone gaining rapid popularity… People will also search more about these popular people or things, on the web.. As the number one search engine, Google will receive most of these search queries! So, who else can make a better report on what we searched through the whole year?

As I said earlier, Google will make a great report at the end of every year which includes, the Fastest Rising Topics, Fastest Falling Topics, Popular News Heading, Fastest Rising People, Fastest Rising Translations, etc. Not only that, they have also created an interactive map which will tell you how people searched about the top 5 global events (in this year they are : World Cup, Olympics, Haiti Earthquake, Oil Spill and Ash Cloud) with time.

They have also created a video highlighting all the trending topics. This three minute video will give you a perfect overview of the year 2010 which is slowly fading beneath the horizon! Watch it and have one last look at 2010 before you leave it!
