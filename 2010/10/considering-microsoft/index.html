Video : Considering Microsoft?

Oct 19th, 2010 | Comments

When did Microsoft start to hate free and open source software? To tell you the truth, they got really scared when they first heard those two words! Free means the total opposite of Microsoft. So, the success of free and opensource market is a big threat to Microsoft.. But, that doesn’t mean that you need to be mean!

Recently, the MS Office team released a video to attack Open Office.org. Open Office is an open source office suit which is distributed free of charge. Speaking about the video, it sure is a really cool video (if you speak about the animations ans stuff..).. But most of it hides the real truth!

At first, they say that open source software have less support! If you get Microsoft’s software.. There are only few developers trapped in their offices. But open source software have thousands of developers all around the world. Some may ask for you to pay in order to get support, but what about the others? I don’t know about you, but even when I search in Google to solve my problems, I get more answers for my Linux based questions rather than for windows based ones. There’s a huge community out there to give us free and reliable support! Have you ever realized how many people are there to just help you to solve Ubuntu problems? Just search in Google or go to their forums and you’ll see what im talking about.

They have mentioned about a problem with MACROS in open office. Some people say that this is the only true argument that they have shown in this video. Actually I can’t say much about it because Im not a big office fan!

They keep on saying that OpenOffice is unstable and inconsistent without any solid proofs. They have also mentioned that Open Source Always Costs!!! Ok, it can cost you to get some employees having open source knowledge. But, if you use proprietary software, there also you have to pay for employees who have knowledge in that software and as a bonus.. you also have to pay for your software!

Ever since Richard Stallman brought the idea of free software, Microsoft and the other proprietary leaders stood against the whole idea. Sometimes they said that open source is piracy and some times they said open source means communism.. And now they are saying that open source always costs and doesn’t have enough supports ?

I know most of you might have a lot to share after watching this video. But unfortunately Microsoft won’t allow us to comment on that video because they know that they are wrong! They know that they are going to get a lot of criticism!

Until today I had at least a small respect towards Microsoft because, thats where I first started. And still I have Windows 7 and Linux running together.. But these actions will dissolve that respect little by little! What do you think?
