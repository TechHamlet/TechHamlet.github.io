When we started TechHamlet.com, the only thing we had in our minds is to be the best! Now we have come a very long way for almost an year and now we have laid a strong base to our big project. At this time, its impossible to stop us and we will never stop! For all these months we have done a very big job on our own with few active, but dedicated authors who were able to make our dreams a success! So, now its time to increase our power and become the biggest blog in the world! To do that task.. we need your help! Will you help us?

As I said earlier, we worked for about an year to put up a strong foundation to our big project! Actually, we started this company to serve the whole tech world with our best. Most of you might think that we only want to become the best blog.. But there are more stuff in our minds and the blog is only a start! Everything big should start with something small right? Well, we started this blog to build a great community around us which was suggested by my friend Sahan Serasinghe, so it will be easy for us to go ahead fast. Things worked perfectly and now we have a good community around us which gets bigger everyday.

You might have seen that we have released a small software recently which is a very simple anti distraction text editor (Mini Edit Beta ( A Distraction Free Editor ). For these projects to succeed, we need more time and help we could get. So, we have decided to get some help with our blog so we can concentrate more on our other projects.

Today when I was talking with my friend Siddartha (From GadgetCage.com) I learnt how he was getting so many articles for his blog from many guest writers. So, I asked him for permission to use that trick on my blog because I also need more help! Fortunately, I have very good friends and he gave me all the details and allowed me to do it in TechHamlet.com!

Now, this is what im going to do :

We will allow you to write guest posts to our blog and give you the chance to get all the ad-sense revenue generated from your own articles! Not only that, we have also decided to select the best writer in each month and reward him with $20! This contest will continue until we decide to officially end it! But don’t worry, it might continue for years and the prize money will get bigger and bigger!

What you’ll get from this great opportunity :

  • 100% Ad-sense revenue from the posts you write

  • $ 20 Prize for the best writer of the month

  • Back-links and traffic from a PR 4 blog which have a very good reputation in search engines.

How do we choose the top writer :

  • The author should write at-least 5 articles per month to be eligible for the prize

  • 5 Points for each article

  • 1 Point for every comment he gets (Only useful comments will be considered. The comments from the author will not be calculated)

  • 2 Points for every Re-tweet, Digg and FB share the posts get. (Shares from the author will not be calculated)

Here’s how to participate :

What to write?

So, what are you waiting for? Head straight to our contact form (http://www.techhamlet.com/contact-us/) and apply to be a guest blogger for TechHamlet.com!
