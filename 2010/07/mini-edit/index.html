A Great Distraction Free Writer to Keep You Glued to Your Writing

Jul 13th, 2010 | Comments

Think that you want to write an essay, a news paper article or a blog post in your computer. So, you go to your PC, switch it on.. Check your e-mails.. write a part of the essay, Chat with your friends.. write another part of the essay, Read some news.. write some more of the essay… Will this actually work?

I know if I do like this, I won’t be able to write my article within that day! Now this is where we need a Distraction free writer to help us concentrate more on our writing. Specially if your doing some big writing like a blog post, a big letter, or a big essay, you’ll really need to concentrate harder! The harder you concentrate, the better your results will be!

But, most of us does a lot of work with our computers so when we sit in-front of it.. we’ll even forget about what we came for! Now, if you want to study hard or do a good meditation, most of us will go to a quiet place which have less distractions so we can concentrate hard on our work. So.. can’t we do the same in the computer?

Why not? I have already used some great apps in Linux which will give you a good distraction free writing experience. These special writers will try to make us focus on writing by taking up the whole screen and allowing us only to WRITE!

But, I wasn’t able to find a good distraction free writer in Windows which can work great like the one I used in Linux (PyRoom). So, I decided to build my own small distraction free editor to focus more on writing!

This is how the idea about Mini Edit came into my mind! Mini Edit is an extremely small software with a very simple interface which will allow you only to write. The editor will take up your whole screen allowing you to concentrate more on your writing. There are only two buttons in the interface which will allow you to minimize and close the editor. To do the rest of the work, you have to use your own keyboard which is described at the bottom of the editor!

Mini Edit will not only allow you to write, but it will also allow you to format your text. Most of the distraction free editors won’t allow you to do this, but our editor will currently allow you to change the font, size and the color of your text! So, the text will be saved in the .rtf (Rich Text) format. It will also allow you to save in the normal .txt format, but it will remove all the formatting!

This little software is still on the Beta stage which means its still under development! But you can use it for your day to day work without any fear! The up coming versions will have lots of more cool feature which you’ll love to have when you want to write in peace!
