Want to Sell Your Old Mobile to the Best Buyer?

Jul 8th, 2010 | Comments

If you buy a mobile today, tomorrow it might be out dated! The mobile world is innovating day by day. Not only the mobile world.. the whole tech world is changing very rapidly! So, if you want to buy a new mobile because your old one is out dated or its broken, are you going to just throw away your old mobile? Don’t you know that there are lots of people ready to buy your old mobile?

Its completely true! There are lots of people around the world who buy old phones and recycle them. People like FoneHub and Fone Bank will allow you to do this job through the net. So, isn’t it great to sell your old mobile rather than just throwing it away?

It will not only give some extra cash to your hands, but it will also help to protect the environment. Do you know that your mobile phone contains toxic chemicals such as arsenic, beryllium, copper or lead which might harm the environment? Everyday millions of mobile phones come out of the factories and thousands of old phones were thrown out. What will happen if the toxic from all these phones start to contaminate our environment? Actually its already heavily contaminated and if we go like this soon our world will be a dead zone! So, these people are doing a very great job in recycling our phones and we must help them! After-all, they also pay you for helping them!

As I said earlier, there are lots of old mobile recyclers around the world, but each one might offer a different price! Some may offer as double as the others! So, how to know whose offering the best price for our old mobiles? By going to each and every site and jotting down their prices? A bit hard isn’t it?

This is where mobile phone recycling comes in. SellMyMobile.com is a UK based site which will greatly simplify your work by giving you the opportunity to select the best price from the best mobile recyclers!

All you got to do is just type in the model of your mobile and press enter! After that, SellMyMobile.com will go through all the top mobile recyclers and show you their :

  • Prices. By default this list is sorted so that the people who offers the highest price will be shown at top.

  • The price difference from the lowest offer that they have.

  • The average user rating of the recyclers. Some recyclers might offer a good price, but they might offer you a very bad service. Some might even refuse to pay you when you send the phone! Who knows what crooks we have in this world. But this user rating feature will allow the users to rate the recyclers. So, if the recyclers have a very bad reputation, they will also have a very bad rating. This will greatly help us to pick a good and trustworthy buyer.

  • The features they offer (Free post, Sending the Postage and Vouchers).

  • The payment method (Via PayPal, Cheques or Credit Cards).

So, what else do you expect? To tell you the truth, SellMyMobile.com is doing a really great job in helping us to find some extra cash and to save our planet! So, what do you think? Do you have an old mobile which you wish to sell and earn some cash? If so, head straight to SellMyMobile.com and start selling!
