Itomo.org Needs Some Tech Geeks to Guest Blog!

Apr 9th, 2010 | Comments

As the title suggests our friend Pro Vamsi at Itomo.org need authors for his growing blog. He needs writers specifically with knowledge on the world of gadgets that are springing up now on a daily basis, so that they can cover up an area which we’ve neglected so far.

Provamsi, also guarantees some guest articles (not specifically as many as you submit) to your blog in return to the favor done to his blog. He will write the guest article himself on the niche given by you.

Itomo.org is a pretty descent, and posting on this site will give you a chance to get free back links to your own blog! These links can surely generate some good traffic to your blog.  Since they are ready to offer 100% adsense revenue from the post you posted, the back link of your blog would be a nofollow link.

So, here is the overall concept. You write few articles for itomo.org which would be a gadget review or some news related to gadgets. In return you get guest article from provamsi, a link and 100% adsense revenue sharing. So that’s the news.

So, people who are interested in joining itomo.org as a contributor are most welcome to follow the link below:

  • http://www.itomo.org/contact
