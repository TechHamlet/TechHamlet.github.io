Win $200 Worth Advertising Space From 10 High PR Sites

Apr 15th, 2010 | Comments

Another opportunity has arisen for the ones who seek to expand their blog’s reader base and to achieve greater heights in the blogging world. It is with pleasure we tell you that one of our brothers at arms are organizing a competition where the winners will be allowed to advertise their blog in high page ranked sites. TechMaish.com is taking this new approach to encourage bloggers and to give them an opportunity to reach the top, every blogger’s desire.

Getting into details now, the competition is all about a package of advertising space for the winner to advertise their blog on other sites and to get web traffic and backlinks from them. In the end, a banner of 125* 125 dimensions will be placed on each of the sponsoring sites in their respective ad spaces. Specifically the prizes are as follows.

There are 10 sponsoring blogs so each one of them will be contributing one 125 x 125 ad space to create one bulk of a huge prize list. So here it goes :

1) Prizes for first place.

The victor will get 4 Advertisement Slots from 4 High Page Rank Blogs + FAQPAL.com.

  1. One 125×125 Banner from JohnPaulaguiar.com (PR 4 Blog)

  2. One 125×125 Banner from TechHamlet.com (from us) (PR 4 Blog)

  3. One 125×125 Banner from Tricksdaddy.com (PR 4 Blog)

  4. One 125×125 Banner from ThesoftHelp.com (PR 4 Blog)

Bonus:– One 125×125 OR 300×100 Banner from FAQPAL.com (PR 5 Blog Post Submission Website)

2) Prizes for second place.

  1. One 125×125 Banner from MyBlog2Day.com (PR 3 Blog)

  2. One 125×125 Banner from TechCats.net (PR 3 Blog)

  3. One 125×125 Banner from GadgetCage.com (PR 3 Blog)

3) Prizes for third place.

  1. One 125×125 Banner from eblogtip.com (PR 3 Blog)

  2. One 125×125 Banner from NetChunks.com (PR 3 Blog)

  3. One 125×125 Banner from BloggingJunction.com (PR 3 Blog)

Here’s what you’ve to do! A short and sweet explanation of how the point system works :

  • Compile a post about the contest in your blog. Do mention the prize list and link it to contest page.

  • Subscribe to RSS feed from TechMaish.com (20 points)

  • Tell your friends and followers at Facebook and twitter about this exciting proposition!! (20 points)

  • Post a comment at TechMaish.com’s contest page with all contact information and verification details. (10 points)

The game will be on from April 8th – May 8th. On the day after the immediate finish of the competition (May 9th) a winner will be chosen through Random.org . Selection is to be done by comments posted. Thus do ensure to complete all four steps to ensure your complete victory.

Rules and regulations :

Bloggers must fully comply with the rules of competition. If contents of the blog are unethical, duplicate or illegal it will be a sorry for you. Thus blogs with only quality content will be allowed in the game.

Have any problems or doubts? You can contact TechMaish.com via “techmaish (@) gmail.com”. Hurry up and do join now!! Fame awaits you at TechMaish.com.

Contest URL : http://www.techmaish.com/200-worth-contest-advertise-your-blog-on-10-high-pr-blogs-for-free/
