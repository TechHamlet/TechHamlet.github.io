Here Comes Promoted Tweets...

Apr 14th, 2010 | Comments

April 13th was a special date for the twitter staff. On that day they unveiled plans for their new ad platform called “Promoted Tweets” . It was stated in twitter blog that the new feature will be “  non-traditional, easy and make a ton of sense for Twitter”. The concept was unveiled yesterday at an AdAge digital conference by twitter  COO Dick Costolo.

First phase of promoted tweets will be launched with a few advertising partners that include  Best Buy, Bravo, Red Bull, Sony Pictures, Starbucks, and Virgin America. Meanwhile twitter promises more are to come. Will it be another ad spammage like how it presently is on Facebook sidebar, is yet unknown. But twitter has explicitly conveyed hopes in making these “Promoted Tweets” appear in our time lines. So the user can get to know interesting information about the sponsoring companies. Interesting or not we’ll see in future.

As of now “Promoted Tweets” are visible in our searches. Also it was stated, in the Q & A session that followed “ all Promoted Tweets are organic Tweets, there is not a single “ad” in our Promoted Tweets platform that isn’t already an organic part of Twitter” sounds promising but yet doesn’t make me bubbly about what’s happening, for many users have expressed their naught about what’s happening. At this time twitter is checking the resonance of the promoted tweets to see whether it’s getting popular among the users. That’s all we know by now. Stay tuned for more news!
