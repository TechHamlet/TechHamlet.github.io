Tech Hamlet 7.0 : New Look for the New Year!

Apr 14th, 2010 | Comments

These days we celebrate our Sinhala and Tamil new year in Sri Lanka! In this season, people clean their houses, buy new cloths and try their best to get ready for the new year! People are more exited than in the January new year! So… we also thought that we should clean our site and give it a new look for the new year!

This is the 7th version of TechHamlet.com! I think you all can remember that we changed our looks in January for the new year and our birthday! That change was one of the most successful changes that we have done to this blog. It solved many problems like some plugin compatibilities and speed! But, recently, it also started to give some major problems. The site looked very clean, but the speed the speed is really slow! And it also started to conflict with some of our plugins, so we made a change!

This time we have switched back to ElegantThemes. I think this is their very first clean wordpress theme. After doing some changes, we were able to fit it perfectly for our site! Its clean, elegant and also really fast! But, when you look at the earlier theme, you might think that its much faster because it didn’t had much fancy stuff! But, the truth is, its much more complicated and heavy!

Not only the theme, we have cut down some of the useless plugins that we had, and installed some new plugins to power up the site! Specially, we added W3 Total Cache to handle caching minifying.

And you might have noticed that we have done some major changes to our comment system! Yesterday, our friend Ramkumar (at http://www.bytechip.com) told us about the Disqus comment system, which seems to be pretty cool. Earlier we used Intense Debate to manage our comments, but it simply ruined all of our comments, so switched to wordpress default comments! But, this time, our new theme had a big and an ugly comment system, so just thought of testing out Disqus. It seems to be working fine!

Remeber I said this is our new year? So, im going to use this post to wish every one a happy new year! Wish you all a very happy and a prosperous new year!
