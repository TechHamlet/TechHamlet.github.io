What is this contest?

This contest is about commenting, and forum posting. Its easy, this is how you’ll get points :

  • 1 point for one comment

  • 1 point for replying to a post in the forum

  • 2 points for posting a new topic in the forum

The contest will end at the end of this month (March) and the person with the highest number of points will be handsomely rewarded!

What are the prizes?

  • A 1 year free license of the world’s best anti-virus (with the complete internet security suit) : BitDefender Internet Security 2010! by Mad Geek (at http://beingpc.com/)

+

  • WinX DVD Ripper Platinum (worth $39.95) by Debasis Pradhan (at http://techchunks.com/)

+
