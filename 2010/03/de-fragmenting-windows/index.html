Windows : Give a Booster to Your PC by De-fragmenting It!

Mar 16th, 2010 | Comments

Everyone knows that Windows PCs need frequent maintenance to get the maximum performance out of them. That’s why we use various system utilities. De-fragment tools are also one of the most important tools which will help you to maintain an error free and a faster computer!

When you use your system, you may write files to your disks, delete them or edit them frequently. Some times even without your knowledge, your system will do these functions in the background. In your hard disks, files are stored in small clusters, so a normal file will be written into several clusters. When you write, delete or modify your files frequently, these files gets fragmented! That means, if you have one directory, one file of that directory, will be written in one place, and another will be written in another place of the disk. Now, to access this directory, your hard disk needs to spin madly, to get to these files!

I think you understood the above explanation. Its a small version of a huge explanation! To learn more about De-fragmentation go to : http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Defragmentation

Now, I think you got the picture! De-fragmenting your hard disk may greatly help you to increase the performance of the whole system. So, its recommended to De-Fragment your disks at least once a week!
