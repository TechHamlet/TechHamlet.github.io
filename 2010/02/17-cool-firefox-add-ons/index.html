17 Cool Firefox Add-ons That You Must Taste

Feb 23rd, 2010 | Comments

I know most of you are Firefox users! Most of you must be using Add-ons to improve your browser’s capabilities! But some of you might not! So, for all of you we have some good news. We have selected some of the best Firefox plug-ins just for your your browser! Want to test them out?

Weave Sync 1.0.1

Synchronize your bookmarks, history, tabs and passwords wherever you go. Whether you use Firefox on your phone, laptop, or desktop, securely access all your data.

Adblock Plus 1.1.3

Ever been annoyed by all those ads and banners on the internet that often take longer to download than everything else on the page? Install Adblock Plus now and get rid of them.

WOT – Safe Browsing Tool

Web of Trust warns you about risky sites that cheat customers, deliver malware or send spam. Millions of members of the WOT community rate sites based on their experience, giving you an extra layer of protection when browsing or searching the Web.

Personas Plus 1.5.1

Free, easy-to-install “skins” for Firefox. Choose from over 60,000 cool designs, including art from Harry Potter and Bob Marley. Join over 8 million people from around the world who are asking themselves: “What Will My Browser Wear Today?”

FoxTab 1.3

3D in your browser! FoxTab brings innovative 3D functionality to your Firefox.

New! Top Sites for FoxTab (aka Speed Dial).

DownThemAll! 1.1.8

The first and only download manager/accelerator built inside Firefox!

Feedly 2.10

A magazine-like startpage. A fast and stylish way to read and share the content of your favorite sites and services. Provides seamless integration with Google Reader, Twitter, Delicious, YouTube and Amazon.

Firebug 1.5.0

Firebug integrates with Firefox to put a wealth of development tools at your fingertips while you browse. You can edit, debug, and monitor CSS, HTML, and JavaScript live in any web page…

BetterPrivacy 1.47

Ever wondered why you are still tracked though you tried everything to prevent it?

BetterPrivacy is a safeguard which protects from usually not deletable LSO’s on Google, YouTube, Ebay…

Read It Later 2.0.3

Read It Later allows you to save pages of interest to read later. It eliminates cluttering of bookmarks with sites that are merely of a one-time interest.

Google Shortcuts

Display all of your Google Services as buttons just next to your addressbar or anywhere you like it.

Download Statusbar

View and manage downloads from a tidy statusbar – without the download window getting in the way of your web browsing.

NoScript 1.9.9.47

The best security you can get in a web browser!

Allow active content to run only from sites you trust, and protect yourself against XSS and Clickjacking attacks.

WiseStamp – Email Signature

WiseStamp- Empower your email signatures in Gmail, Google Apps, Yahoo! Mail, Hotmail or AOL…,Multiple signatures ,Auto insert ,Add IM & Social profiles (Twitter, Facebook, Digg, LinkedIn…) ,Add Feeds ,Easily add Logo or HTML & more…Get it now!

TwitterBar 2.8.1

Post to Twitter from your address bar.

Winner of Mozilla Mobile Add-ons Challenge!

Cooliris 1.11.6

The Cooliris 3D Wall — Simply the fastest and most stunning way to browse photos and videos from the Web or your desktop. Effortlessly scroll an infinite “3D Wall” of your content from Facebook, Google Images, YouTube, Flickr, and hundreds more.

AddThis 3.0.0

AddThis for Firefox is the best add-on to make sharing and bookmarking simple. Have all your favorite web 2.0 social networking, bookmarking, blogging, and e-mail services at your fingertips. Share any page, anytime, with anyone.
