3 Cheers! We Have Finally Come Under the 1000 Top Websites in Our Home Land Sri Lanka!

Feb 24th, 2010 | Comments

Ever since we changed to the new domain I kept an eye on our Alexa ranks everyday! And today, when I check our ranks…. we have finally reached the top 1000 websites in our mother land Sri Lanka!

I think most of you know that the home land of TechHamlet.com is a small paradise in the Indian ocean called Sri Lanka! A country full of talented minds and victorious people!

I have seen many blogs in the net maintained by our fellow Sri Lankans, but almost all of those blogs are written in our native language (Sinhala), so they are closed to the international community!

But we started our blog in complete English to take the name of our country to the World! So, at first, when we started our blog, we got most of the traffic from USA&t=h) and Sri Lanka is nowhere in the ranks!

But, in last December (2009) things started to change. India&t=h) (our closest neighbor) managed to beat USA and become our number one traffic source. Since then, Sri Lanka also started to give traffic to us. After we changed our name to Tech Hamlet, Sri Lanka became the second best traffic source of TechHamlet.com.

After that our ranks in Sri Lanka started to rise until we reached 1000 top web sites in our country! And now its 850!

Now we have three more targets :

  1. To come under the Top 10 websites in Sri Lanka (to be the first, we need to beat Google, which will take a lot of time!)

  2. To come under the Top 1000 websites in India! (Currently, its 6,656)

  3. To come under the Top 10,000 web sites in the World! (Currently we are at 119,829. I think this target will be achieved really soon!)

To tell the truth, to get these ranks (not only Sri Lankan rank), Indian bloggers are the people who helped us most. So I want to thank them and all my Sri Lankans who have helped my site even by visiting us! Thank you very much my friends for making our blog a success! If you have a blog on your own I wish that your ranks will be even better than mine!
