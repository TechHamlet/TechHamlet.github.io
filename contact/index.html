For Guest Articles :

Now its really hard to submit guest posts to our site. If you are trying to submit guest posts just to get links, to your clients or for your products, please do not even try sending your post. We reject all! We accept only posts from people who really love to write something. If you think you have a good title to write, write it in Markdown and send it to us. We will check it and publish it if its worthy! Only send articles that are related to the following categories :

Please do not send any article related to blogging, SEO, marketing, and any other similar categories! They will be rejected!
