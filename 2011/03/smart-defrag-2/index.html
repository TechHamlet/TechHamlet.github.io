Smart Defrag 2 : The Smartest Disk Defragger Is Finally Here!

Mar 21st, 2011 | Comments

Don’t you think you can speedup your machine even more? Don’t you think your machine can survive much longer without getting some new hardware? Didn’t any famous system utility help you by tweaking your system, cleaning junk files and even by cleaning the registry? Then, the best choice is to try to treat your hard disk! You’ll get surprised when you see how fast your machine can perform after giving it a good defrag! Have any idea on what to use for the defragmentation? Why don’t you try Smart Defrag?

Every Windows machine mess things up when they read and write data on to your hard disk. One folder can be stored all around the disk. So, it takes a lot of time to go through the whole disk and read that folder. But, what if we keep them close together? That’s exactly what a Disk Defragment tool will do. It will carefully organize the files in your disks and make them easy to access.

There are a lot of premium quality defrag tools in the world which offers top class disk defragmentations for a big price. But, now, free tools have started to conquer the market and IObit’s Smart Defrag is at the top! With the latest version of Smart Defrag, its ready to challenge the premium market.

The newest feature which made Smart Defrag more powerful is the Boot time Defragmentation. Normally, when your system is running, you can’t change or move your system files. So, when you run a normal defrag utility, it will just skip these files. A boot time defragemntation process will defrag your system files during the system boot (before your OS is fully loaded). But, most of the premium defraggers have this feature. According to my knowledge, the only free tool which have this boot time defragment feature is Defraggler. But, already Smart Defrag 1.0 have managed to come to the top even without this feature. So, now that they have it, this will make them even more powerful.

Not only that, this latest version have a brand new interface which have two major skins, black and white. IObit have also managed to increase the defragment speeds and even reduce the load on system resources. Not only on auto defrag which will automatically defrag your disks in the real-time, even during a full system defrag, this little app won’t take that much of resources.

This small and lightweight tool have already managed to get featured on Cnet’s home page! So, there’s no doubt that this little app really is the number one when it some to disk defragmenting your drives. So, what are you waiting for? It’s completely free and offers premium quality features… What else do you want?

Download : http://www.iobit.com/iobitsmartdefrag.html
