Have you formated your computer but forgot to recover your data? Did you accidentally delete a very important file? Did your brother delete your school project which you have worked for months? Well, these are very pathetic situations which will give you the strength to throw some part of your machine across the room… Am I wrong? I don’t think so. Everyone gets frustrated when they lose their important data… But before throwing your computer parts, shall we try to recover these data?

Most of you might have already given up hope when you see your data was deleted. But the truth is, they are not gone for ever! Not at least for some time… The trick is with our lazy hard disks. Normally, when you create some data, it will store the data in a small place in the hard disk (a cluster). But that won’t make the data accessible. To do that, the hard disk stores the address of that pice of data and the associated clusters in a small table. This is called a reference. When you delete the data, the disk will only delete the reference and forget about the data! The data will be still on the same cluster. But, if the disk decided to write another file in the same place, then it’ll remove it and add the new data. So, you have a great chance of recovering your data before its overwritten!

Of course a normal user cannot recover these files without any help. That’s why we have iolo Search and Recover. This small piece of software will help you to recover your lost data in many situations… Its not only limited to hard disks. It can recover data from many devices such as Hard Disks, CDs, DVDs, Cameras, Music Players, Memory Cards, USB Devices, Firewire Devices and many more kinds of data storage devices.

This small app is very user friendly and will take you through step by step wizards in order to recover your data. It also have special scanners designed specially to recover important file types like Images, Songs, Videos, and also to recover your lost emails!

Sometimes you might think that a damaged, malfunctioning or a formatted disk cannot be recovered. But iolo can also recover these kinds of disks. Remember, if you have put a critical format like a low-level format, your chances are very low! (I think a low level format will wipe out all of your chances.)

Another special feature you have in Search and Recover is that you can recover all the data from a specified folder,driver,device, CD or a DVD in just one click. The software will scan the whole place and automatically recover all the data to one single folder. Their spacial StrongScan technology can recover data even after years of deleting them! So, its a very strong pice of software which will be very useful for you. Even if you don’t need it now, you might want it in the future… You’ll never know when you’ll lose your data!
