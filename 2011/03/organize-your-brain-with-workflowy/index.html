Organize Your Brain With WorkFlowy

Mar 26th, 2011 | Comments

The ultimate to-do list is here to help you out to organize your brain. Workflowy which offers a to-do list that is very simple is an excellent example of an organizing list. A simple principle is used by Workflowy to help you out with organizing your brain. Workflowy helps you to concentrate on what’s the most important at the time and leave out the least important for the later work. Comparing with other to-do list tools like ta-da-list, RTM and Todo.ly , Workflowy out powers them all with a simple design and organizing techniques.

When you first signup to Workflowy account you will be strangely taken into a black page with just the Workflowy logo on top. But believe me this black page can be turned into your life list by simply using imagination of your own brain. With it you can organize your personal life, projects, goals, commitments and many more. Sky is your limit. Best part of Workflowy is that how big your list grows is up to you and you can always be at because workflowy only shows you the selected items child elements. More detail guide of Workflowy is available via the video below.

  • Adding up to this video I polished up some the basic list

  • A list to contain your Goals broken to weekly, monthly, yearly

  • A list that contain things that contains routine tasks

  • A list with all the items to collect including movies, songs, anime, Knowledge, experiences

  • A list that contains Ideas falling into suggestions, hunches, and Milestones too

  • A list that features all your currently working projects

  • A list that contains things you are supposed to do.

You can customize the internal items the way you want to. Add catchy names to your lists that you will remember. This is important because when your list grows big, trust me you would like friendly names.

And again comparing with other to-do applications like RTM(Remember the Milk) Workflowy doesn’t have intimidating menus with mountains of features. It’s just a black paper. And above all you are not troubled to add completion times, dates which makes all other to-do lists troublesome. Try it out and see the difference. Your organizational skills and productivity increase very fast.

Few must know facts about Workflowy

  • Keyboard shortcuts are available for easy use

  • Searching is available

  • Hiding items that are finished is also available

And below is a list analysis of available features and not avilable ones

Pros

  • No predefined categories, or limitations

  • High usability

  • Exporting as text available

Cons

  • Offline support is not available

  • List sharing unavailable

  • List tagging is not available

As I suggested earlier, the best thing about Workflowy is simplicity. That is why I believe that sharing, tagging features are not much of use. If you want these complex features try something like RTM and Ta-da list. I can remember a phrase one of my friends used to say. Simplicity is genioucity. With this I also like to complement Albert Einsteins Quote about a genius.

” Any intelligent fool can make things bigger and more complex… It takes a touch of genius - and a lot of courage to move in the opposite direction.” Â - Albert Einsteins

Go ahead and try out Workflowy today. Sign-up is as simple as their list. And drop us a line how you made your list.
