Happy Birthday Dear Twitter! Now You Are 5 Years Old!

Mar 23rd, 2011 | Comments

It’s Twitter’s 5th birthday and we all are celebrating it. 5 years before the very first tweet was sent by Jack Dorsey with the message saying – “just setting up my twttr”

The twitter has revolutionized the way people look into many things and People all around the world use it for different purposes. Twitter which was back then has changed up to the lifestyles of many people. We use twitter to learn, gain, build community, have fun, seek, and for many other things. Twitter is a great lifestyle companion that might help us in our voyage of life.

Twitter has more than 190 million people now and our prediction is that this number will triple in the world because many users a trying out this extremely awesome service.

What’s more great Twitter has innovated to build a new website and a video to celebrate their birthday. The video contains icons of various fields talking about how they use twitter. This list features.

The new website tweet has opened up is http://discover.twitter.com/

In my personal experience when I drop a tweet saying good night to everyone there will always be an @ reply by someone greeting back same goes for morning and other times of the day. Posting blog posts and quotes to the tweeter profile is great thing but remember tweet is also a great tool to build community. Relationships matter a lot in this vast globe. So don’t stay back when others great good morning. Drop a reply and tell the world you care.

Twitter has broken the social sphere of languages and supports many languages. I am currently learning Japanese and Spanish from my Japanese and Spanish friends on Twitter. Twitter was used in emergency situations too and many tweets have helped to reduce damage in many place. The hashtag #preyforjapan has been very popular on twittersphere for many days and it will likely be there for many days.
