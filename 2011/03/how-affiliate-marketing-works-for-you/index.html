How Affiliate Marketing Works for You

Mar 5th, 2011 | Comments

A process where you target products, promote it and then make sales to earn commission. It is not that much easy but you need to start in right direction and then spend time on right promotional ways. Basically it involves 3 basic approaches: 1. Product Selection, 2. Product Appearance and 3rd Product Promotion.

The 3 steps can be easily described in a few words but practically it needs lots of work. The first step is base for the rest of the 2, if you have selected the right product then half of the work is done. If the product you selected will not attract people, then all your efforts made on promotion will get ruined.

This is very important to conduct a research for product selection. Various tools and channels are available through which can analyze products. For example Clickbank Market Place, CBengine, CBtrends etc..

You can research in different places but you need to keep a few things in your mind when selecting a product.

1) Landing Page

Check the landing page of the product official website, whether it is attractive, have sufficient information, testimonials and bonuses or not.

2) Alternatives

What are the alternatives available for the product you are going to promote. What price competitors charge, what benefits they deliver and what type of promotional strategy they use.

3) Trend

Analyze the trend of the product, who are promoting the product, how they are promoting, read user comments, ask in forums and if possible try the product by your self.

After knowing your product (KYP) you can easily decide whether to go for it or not. In short when you perform a SWOT Analysis (Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities and Treats) of your product you can get a clear idea about the product and can start work on it.

So make sure that the product you are going to promote is worth the money people will be investing on it and you will see the result in shape of lots of sales.
