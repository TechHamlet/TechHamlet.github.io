Experience the Beauty of the Web : Internet Explorer 9 Is Here!

Mar 15th, 2011 | Comments

Finally, the most awaited Internet Explorer 9 is ready to give you a more beautiful web! Just now, Microsoft have released the final version of IE 9 to the public. With more speed, more security, and with a better look, IE 9 managed to get a lot of downloads during its beta stages… Actually, it got 40 million beta downloads! So, how many downloads will the final release get?

This is an era where many people start to refuse Internet Explorer due to its slow performance, less security and less support for latest web technologies. Most of the other browsers have already released newer versions and improved themselves to suit the modern web. But, IE is way behind of them! But, IE 9 might change everything!

Remember how they fixed the mistakes of Windows Vista? They released Windows 7 which user’s can’t refuse at all! Now, they are going to do the same thing for the Internet Explorer.

The Internet Explorer 9 will now come with a completely new look. Remember Google Chrome’s minimalistic look? Well, now that concept is coming to IE. An interface designed to give more priority for browsing.

One of the biggest features in IE 9 is the pined tabs. In your Windows 7 taskbar, can you see the pined apps which will give you quick easy access to all your favorite applications? Well, Internet Explorer 9 will let you pin websites just like this! So, you can access your favourite websites just like accessing your favourite apps!

IE 9 have also made a significant job in supporting hardware acceleration and improving graphics support. They have also added a brand new JavaScript engine called Chakra to increase the speed of your browsing.

Another interesting feature on 9 is the Hang recovery. Earlier, when a site goes down due to a never-ending script, your whole browser will go down. But, now, only that tab will go down saving rest of the browser.They have also done a lot to improve the security of the browser by adding features like improved active x filtering nd tracking protection.

Remember that I said the current IE doesn’t support new web technologies? Well, this latest version support HTML 5 and CSS 3 which will take you all the way to the future of the web!

Well, today there’s a big party at Microsoft to celebrate this big event! Just few minutes ago they have released a photo of the big crowd :

So, what do you think? Will IE 9 beat the record? Actually, IE is not the only browser to make new releases… Chrome and Opera both release newer versions of their browsers very recently and Firefox 4 is already on the way! So, can Microsoft stand up to the challenge with this new release? Please feel free to share your ideas with us!
