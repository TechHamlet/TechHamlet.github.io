LIVE Today - Mr. Tumblr vs Mr. Posterous

Mar 10th, 2011 | Comments

Well… Era of blogging has started. Then what’s Tumblr and Posterous doing? Well to make a long story short Tumblr and Posterous are two Blogging services used by many users. These are blogs that are easy to make and maintain. Twitter is a micro blogging platform. But Tumblr and Posterous are in between real blogging platforms like Wordpress and Blogger (Bear in mind Wordpress is now acclaimed to be a Content Management System too). What Posterous and Tumblr do is that they provide a platform for people to add posts, links, images, videos, chats, and many more. It’s kind of like a bank vault that contains a large number of content. Both of these tools are highly used but many users have their own personal opinions. After researching for a while to find which to chose as my client I couldn’t find a lot about both of them. So I reached the radical decision of trying out both of them.

I found a lot about both of the platforms. Let’s start with Tumblr. Tumblr is a great service. Their home page is really simple :

Their page is that simple. You could signup easily and publish your first post on the sly. Then only will they ask if we want to change the theme or add pages. In my opinion it’s great~ Simplicity is Geniocity. They also have 30 reasons why we can love Tumblr. That too is interesting. View them at their homepage.

http://www.tumblr.com
