Apple iPad 2nd Generation Review

Mar 7th, 2011 | Comments

Apple has announced its second generation tablet pc , the iPad 2 . Much thinner and lighter than iPad 1 , iPad 2 adds quite a few welcome features to its first generation counterpart. The 2nd generation tablet pc was announced on March 2nd 2011 and comes in colors of Black and white right from the day 1 of its release which is officially scheduled on 11th march 2011 . If you are looking to buy a iPad , below is a quick review for you .

Apple iPad 2 Features :

More powerful iPad :

iPad 2 is powered with A5 processor which is twice as fast as 1st generation iPad [A4 Processors] . A5 processor comes with dual core chip that can handle graphic intensive processes quite fast , which means faster response from apps and quick navigation .

Faster Safari :

Camera : Facetime on iPad 2

**

iPad 2 comes with two cameras. The front camera is for face time video calling with other apple products with face time feature [iPhone 4 iPod touch 4 and Macbook]. iPad 2 front camera will bring HD video calling to all or face time enabled devices . There is a rear camera too for taking images and switching face time from the first camera to the rear . You can make video calls over WiFi or using Skype .
