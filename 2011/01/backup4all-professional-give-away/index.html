Jan 26th, 2011 | Comments

Are you hundred percent sure about the safety of your data? Are you sure that your hard drive is not going to fail in any moment? Are you sure that your data won’t get accidentally deleted or damaged by a virus? Are you dead sure that no one is going to steal your PC in the next 24 hours? The truth is, no one knows when we will loose our data! That’s why we use backups! So, today we are going to gift you a great software which will allow you to manage your backups easily and in a more reliable manner!

Tell me, haven’t you thought of backing up your data before anything could happen to them? To tell you the truth, most of the people are lazy to take backups. But, when ever something goes wrong, they try to search for recovery software! But, that won’t give you a complete recovery! The best option is to take regular backups. This might be a bit hard if you try to do it manually, but what if some one helps you?

Backup4All might be the best answer for you. This is a very handy software which will take care of your backups and take a huge load off your back! If you’re a lazy user who can’t manually take your backups, you can use this tool to schedule backup tasks so that it will make the backups automatically without your knowledge! Currently, Backup4All supports 4 backup types : full backup, differential backup, incremental backup and mirror backup. If you’re not a power user, then the full backup will server you well.

Think that your PC was stolen or a fire broke up and burned down your house! If you have kept your backups in the same hard drive, then, there’s no way of recovering after such a disaster. But, Backup4All allows you to backup your data to a remote location via FTP or the more secure SFTP protocol. Cool isn’t it?

Not only that, this handy software have many plugins which will allow you to backup and restore settings and configurations of various software such as game saves, browser settings, email settings, etc. Sometimes, it will be hard to take backups when the file is open. But, this won’t stop Backup4All. It can backup open or locked files without any problem. It can also compress and zip your backups even if they are over 4GB in size!
