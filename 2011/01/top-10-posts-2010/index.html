Top 10 Tech Hamlet Posts of 2010!

Jan 2nd, 2011 | Comments

Another year have passed and we have already started working on a fresh new year! But, before we go forward, it might be better to turn around and have one single look at the past. So, now we are going to take you back on a small journey to the past. Buckle up your seat belts and lets ride!

Top 10 Posts of the Year 2010 :

1) Step by Step How To Install Ubuntu 10.04

Last month Canonical have released the latest version of the Ubuntu family called Lucid Lynx (Ubuntu 10.04). Ubuntu is the most famous Linux system in the planet. Not only that, recently, it started its journey to become the famous operating system in the world by beating Microsoft Windows! But, there are many people around the world who don’t know how to install Ubuntu on their systems. So, today we are going to show them how to safely install Ubuntu in their machines!

2) Google Chrome : Save Tabs and Exit

When you try to exit Mozilla Firefox while there are open tabs, they will ask you whether to save them or not. If you save them, they will be loaded automatically the next time we open the browser. So that we can continue what we did earlier! Google Chrome doesn’t have this feature by default. But, a small extension can add this nice feature to Google Chrome as well!

3) Updated : OK, I installed Ubuntu! now what?

What most people used to do is just install any OS and start their work, they don’t care about security…. they don’t care about the integrity of the system….. so the life span of those systems are very small! After some time they get…. viruses…. corrupted files……continues system errors and many more….

4) Test Drive Your Android Applications on Ubuntu in 3 Steps

You own an Android device or ever wanted to test drive some Android applications even if you don’t have a device? Then this is for you! This time we are going to take you for a fascinating ride on running Android applications in your Ubuntu box!

5) Updated : Video : See Whats The Fastest Browser by Yourself!

You might have already chosen the perfect web browser for your day today browsing. The first thing you might have checked must be the speed of that browser… But wait.. Have you ever tried to run a speed test to pick the fastest browser or did you picked it because some one just told you that its the fastest? Today we are also going to show you whats the fastest browser… But we have solid proof!

6) Easy EBook Management with Calibre

Always worried about when you see your ebooks sitting here and there on your PC? Let’s put a full stop to it today. Calibre is a multi-platform, free and open-source ebook library management app that would save you a tons of time. It does not only manage your ebook collection, but also it has some cool features like fetching news from over 225 news sources,converting them to various formats and allows you to sync your collection remotely!

7) What is the Best Firewall for Ubuntu? UFW or Firestarter?

To tell the truth, UFW and Firestarter are not firewalls! They are only the interfaces used to configure the default firewall that comes in Linux! Yesterday, I did some research about these two and found out an interesting fact! Actually, this one fact will help you to choose what is the best!

8) Ubuntu Customization Guide : Purple Boy

Most of the people think that Linux is an un-userfiendly operating system with horrible graphics! Most of them think that Windows have better desktop effects! Well… that’s in the old time… Today its the other way around. Do you know that you can customise a Linux desktop with better effects than windows? Today Im going to show you how to turn your Ubuntu desktop into something you’ll love better than windows!

9) HowTo Try Out the New Ubuntu Font in Your System?

Ubuntu was able to do a lot of contribution to the Linux world in the past few years. Recently, they did a lot of changes to the look of their famous OS like going to a completely different boot screen and a completely different theme to impress their users! But still its not enough for them.. Now they are trying to go for a completely different Font for their famous Linux system!

10) 5 Ways to Avoid Becoming “The Tech Guy”

I always find myself stuck with the guy in the corner of the room at parties that has no clue what ‘this iPod hoopla’ is all about and wants to totally pick my brain of any knowledge I’ve attained around this subject. It gets even better when every visit to the parent’s home turns into the annoying task of virus scans and spyware removal, just to receive the unwanted referrals to all the family friends to perform this exciting task for them as well for FREE.
