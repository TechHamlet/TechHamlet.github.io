Here Lies IE 6 : 2001 - 2014

Jan 23rd, 2011 | Comments

Remember the old days where you used grand father Internet Explorer 6 to browse the web? Well, back then, IE6 is one of the most popular browsers in the world! Today, people are using IE8 and looking forward for IE9! But, still, Microsoft seems to be providing support for IE6! Actually, none of the modern technologies works in IE6! Even the latest IE8 have become a great pain for webmasters, so, why are they supporting IE6?

After going through Microsoft’s site, it seems to be they are supporting our friend IE6 as long as they support XP SP3, and XP SP3 is going to be supported until 2014! Actually, the thing which bothers me is that why would they support an old out of date browser? The security experts are advising us to upgrade our software to the latest version to stay in the safe side. IE6 is a browser which have caused a lot of trouble due to its security holes. But, still Microsoft officially supports it? As I said earlier, even the latest version of the Internet Explorer doesn’t support the latest web technologies, and trust me, its a real headache for webmasters!

I think our friends Ryan Parr and David Stewart have also got their heads screwed up because of the Internet Explorer. They have built a very simple site (ie6death.com) for those who can’t wait till they put a stop to IE6! This site have a big head stone for IE6 and also a big counter which will keep on counting until the end of IE6! If you want to support them, you can also add their badge on your own site! So, what do you think? When IE6 die… Will the whole family of Internet Explorer die out while Firefox, Chrome and Opera come forward to take the throne?
