Convert Your Documents Online With Zoho Viewer!

Jan 13th, 2011 | Comments

The days when you needed to install software for basic office tasks is completely passé. With the advent of web apps you can get them easily done without any hesitation. Zoho Viewer is a nice alternative to Google Docs and doesn’t require you to sign in to their service. Now they have introduced the ability to convert documents online!

Previously Zoh0 was just an online document viewer which did a fairly nice job even offering us the facility to open up documents in public domains apart from uploading documents to it. For instance let’s say that you want to open up presentation which was created by OpenOffice Impress and you want to open it up in Microsoft PowerPoint. You would be fallen into a big trouble as it would be completely pointless. So what are you doing to do? There you have services like Zoho to lend you a helping hand.

Alright, that was an easy thing to open it up in Zoho Viewer. Let’s say that you want to convert the aforementioned presentation to ppt format. What would be the solution? Unless you are geek it’s most likely that you would be just wasting your time searching for online/offline converters for it. Nevertheless, Zoho has introduced their latest invention, by which you can convert almost any office document into the file format you want.

In order to use their service just head over to their Zoho Converter. Then select the file you want to convert and from the drop down menu below that, select what the resulting format you wish it to be. That’s that! After few seconds Zoho would return back and allow you to download the converted file.

The major draw back I found was that currently they don’t allow us to convert files in a public domain. Let’s hope that they will add it soon.

Supported Files

Microsoft Office: doc, docx, xls, xlsx, ppt, pptx, pps; OpenDocument: odt, ods, odp; OpenOffice:sxw, sxc, sxi; Other Formats: rtf, txt, html, csv, tsv

So now you know how to save you life when it comes to converting office documents in a matter of seconds!
