Apple's CEO Steve Jobs Takes a Leave for a Medical Reason!

Jan 17th, 2011 | Comments

Today, Apple’s CEO Steve Jobs, sends a message to his employees mentioning that he’s going to take a leave for a small time! Steve is one of the richest and busiest men in the world. Recently, he might have worked really hard to take Apple to the front line… This is a time where Apple is getting a lot of popularity, a time where they get a lot of work. With the introduction of iPad and iPhone 4, their earnings also went up in a record speed! But, will it remain like that with the absence of their main man?

This is not the first time Steve Jobs took a leave. On 2009 also he have taken a leave for a liver transplant! This time, still we have no idea on what might be causing troubles to his health. Today, he have sent this message to his employees :

Team,

At my request, the board of directors has granted me a medical leave of absence so I can focus on my health. I will continue as CEO and be involved in major strategic decisions for the company.

I have asked Tim Cook to be responsible for all of Apple’s day to day operations. I have great confidence that Tim and the rest of the executive management team will do a terrific job executing the exciting plans we have in place for 2011.

I love Apple so much and hope to be back as soon as I can. In the meantime, my family and I would deeply appreciate respect for our privacy.

Steve

It seems to be he’s asking for complete privacy on his vacation. I hope that he will be ok soon! This leaves two big questions… Will Tim Cook keep the apple without falling like the first time when Steve got a leave? Whats going wrong with our dear Steve? Who knows, soon we will get some answers and untill then, lets wish that our friend will get a fast recovery!
