Google Receives a Big Ice Cream Sandwich!

Oct 15th, 2011 | Comments

Samsung and Google are both waiting to reveal their latest products this month. But, before Android can send their Ice Cream Sandwich to us, they just received a big Ice Cream Sandwich for themselves! A 10 foot tall Sandwich now stands in front of Google’s office!

Believe it or not, Android didn’t had an official Twitter account! Only thing we had is the twitter account of their developer team @AndroidDev. Actually, it’s a bit odd right? Almost everyone use Twitter to send real time updates to their users… Many rely on Twitter to get the latest news! So, Google have just realized this problem and have created the official Android Twitter account @Android to keep us updated.

Now, let’s get back to the topic… Yes Google created the official Android twitter account and everyone started to write about it. That’s why I didn’t mention about it at first. I chose to write about the second tweet they posted through their twitter account. That tweet points to a video which shows how a big delivery truck delivers a 10 foot tall Ice Cream Sandwich to a group of hungry children!

Normally, Google have a habit of erecting statues for each of their Android versions… In the video, you can see a Cupcake, a Gingerbread Man and a Honeycomb representing the earlier versions of Android. Now, they are going to introduce another member to the family on the 19th of October! So, watch this video and get ready!
