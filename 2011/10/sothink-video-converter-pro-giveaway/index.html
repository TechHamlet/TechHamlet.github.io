Update : Sothink Video Converter Pro Giveaway (5 Keys | Worth Over $124)

Oct 7th, 2011 | Comments

Do you want to convert a video you downloaded so that you can put it into your phone? Do you want to convert a video you created to a web friendly format? Do you want to convert a video to reduce its size? Is there any other reason why we need to convert a video? Of course there are plenty of reasons! That’swhy there are a lot of free and premium apps which claims to do this job! Want to pick one for your self? Then keep reading…

As I said earlier, there are plenty of video converters in the market. Also there are a lot of free working converters. But today, we are going to look at a premium audio/video converter which have quite a lot of features if you really want to dive in. You might have already heard about the free Sothink Video converter. You might have already used it… But today, we are going to look at the paid pro version!

As I said earlier, we can use a video converter for many purposes. So, novice users and advanced geeks both need these kinds of tools. A geek needs in-depth customizations while a regular user needs a straightforward way to do his job faster. Sothink have designed their Video converter to suit both these users. At first, the user can just add their videos, select the output mode and hit convert. But, if you are an advanced user, just switch over to the “Advanced” tab and dive in.

Adding videos to this handy converter is very easy. You can add them through the software just like all the other tools or, you can just right click the video in Windows Explorer and select ”Sothink Video converter”.

Actually, we can’t call this tool a simple converter. Need to cut some parts from your video? You can simply right click it and go to the trim option which will let you do some editing. So, no need to install big apps for small editing…

There’s another cool feature which you should know about… Normally, working with videos will take a huge amount of system resources and time. So, if you want to convert several long videos, then your machine will be terribly slow for that time period. But, Sothink have the answer… Go to Tools > Options > Encoding. In here, you can give a lover priority to your conversion so that you will have some resources left for yourself. Or, you can give it high priority and then set the app to shut down the machine when everything is complete… So, you can sleep peacefully while Sothink do the converting!
