Ohm… My! iPhone 5!

Feb 15th, 2011 | Comments

[ad#Dulitha Wijewantha - Lead-in AdSense Text]

That’s right folks! Apple will probably let the iPhone 5 out in this June and the public can get the hang of it. It is said that the iPhone 5 will carry a G at the end, making it the iPhone 5G. iPhone 4 which was released in 2010 didn’t support 4g technology. That is one of the most complaint issues about the iPhone 4. And in the iPhone world there is a huge debate about the two service providers – Verizon and AT&T. The latter service currently experience dropped and failed calls around the giant cities of US. But above all these, Apple is going to improve the hardware of the iPhone 5.

The Key improvements are said to be -

  • Duel mode Qualcomm chip for CPU

  • Implementing NFC (Near Field Communication)

iPhone users are also anticipating the release of the new i OS 5. When iPhone 4 was released in 2010 the public thought of waiting till the iPhone 5 arrived and it is about to come out. Other minor features Apple hopes to include in their latest Gadget are -

  • 4th Generation Video chat that is much faster than the 3rd generation iPhone

  • Scratch and shatter proof screen

  • The increased space model of 64GB

  • Longer battery life of 14 hours 3g talk time

[ad#Dulitha Wijewantha - MidPostAd]

These features might change because you can never tell what Apple will do next! Sony is said to be developing a 8 megapixel camera that over takes the Apple iPhone camera of 5 megapixel. Be ready to purchase an iPhone 5 for it will really worth it for the iPhone fans. And above all, be updated about the war between Verizon and AT&T.
