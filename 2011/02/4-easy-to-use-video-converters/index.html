4 Easy-to-Use Video Converters for Multiple Platforms

Feb 16th, 2011 | Comments

The technology available today allows us to convert video and audio files into a variety of different formats and in multiple platforms. These files can then be played on different devices, including iPods and iPhones.

1) MediaCoder

MediaCoder can convert audio and video files to and from almost all of the major file formats. It has an innovative graphic interface. This software can be used with all browsers and can do a wide range of simple and complicated conversions.

It contains a wide selection of codecs and has many innovative tools. This system is flexible and they are always adding updated and new features to enhance the program. You can also use this video converter to fix files that are incomplete or damaged in some way.

2) HandBrake

HandBrake is an open source converter that is easy to use and will convert a variety of file formats. This software has a variety of setting options that can be customized to your liking. It is easy to use and converting a DVD just takes a couple of clicks.

This is a robust software that allows the user to control and adjust all options. This software can convert all types of file formats to be used on many different platforms. They are always improving this application and now you can include Closed Captioning data with your videos.

3) Format Factory

Format Factory is a user-friendly media converter that is free. With this software, you can convert audio, video and images into different file formats. The layout is designed to make it easy for everyone to be able to use it. The picture converting option includes rotating, tags and zoom tools. This is a nice added feature since most converters only handle video and audio files.

It easily can rip DVDâ€™s and can repair audio and video files that are damaged. It has an Audio Joiner that lets you take two totally separate files and joins them together so you can convert them. Not only can you convert files from most formats, this converter also has the formats of many of the popular portable devices.

4) SUPER Media Converter

The SUPER Media Converter is a basic media converter that very easy to learn. You can quickly learn this system and will be converting multimedia files before you know it. This application is both a converter and a player. It works on all platforms and is a universal video converter.

The application has the ability to convert files for portable devices including iPod, iTunes, Nintendo DS and PS3, just to name a few. You have easy access to a full featured menu with just a click of your mouse. You can process multiple batch files by simply dragging and dropping your files into the converter. The SUPER is an all in one multimedia player and converter that is quick and efficient.

It is quick and easy to convert files using one of these media converters. No matter what platform your computer or mobile device has; you now have the freedom to watch your videos anywhere, anytime.
