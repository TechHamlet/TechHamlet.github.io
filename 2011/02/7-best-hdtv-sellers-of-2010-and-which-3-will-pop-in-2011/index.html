7 Best HDTV Sellers of 2010, and Which 3 Will Pop in 2011

Feb 28th, 2011 | Comments

What is your favorite feature on HDTV? Are you impressed with the clarity of the image? Does the sound projection make you want to buy any particular unit? What about the connectivity features?

HDTV technology (in an abundance) made a regular, profitable appearance in 2010 with units that really impressed consumers. Some LCD units were not only affordable, but were also gadget-friendly, making things like gaming and Internet connectivity an all-around blast for everyone.

Of the HDTVs that made the biggest impressions, here are 7 of the best sellers that topped 2010, and 3 of which are expected to really increase in popularity in 2011.

1. Samsung LN46C650 46-inch LCD HDTV

  • Sold for less than $1,000

  • Impressive display screen, great for gaming features

  • Accepts both digital cameras and flash drives

  • Connectivity for social networking sites

  • Energy compliant

2. Vizio TruLED 240 Hz Internet-ready LCD HDTV

  • Internet-ready TV

  • Check social networking sites and interact online

  • 55-inch direct type LED-backlit

  • LCD HDTV with full 1080p HD resolution (1920 x 1080 pixels)

  • 240Hz refresh rate

  • Smart Dimming intelligently controls LED blocks to produce 10,000,000:1 dynamic contrast ratio

  • Has a detachable base and thumbscrew

  • Bluetooth remote with slide-out QWERTY keyboard

  • Expected to be a featured item in 2011

3. Samsung LN46C650 46-Inch 1080p 120 Hz LCD HDTV (Black)

  • Display at 720p, but is a very hardy unit that delivers

  • Designed to coincide with an iPod

  • Interlinking feature for AV devices and Blu-Ray disc players

  • Supports camera memory cards to view photos

  • Expected to be a featured item in 2011

4. Toshiba 55UX600U 55” LED HDTV

  • Can view Toshiba Net TV on-demand

  • Movies displayed in 1080p high-definition; 5.1 surround sound

  • Connects to social networking sites

  • Game mode feature adjusts image for optimal gaming viewing

  • A 55-inch screen with a price tag around $1,300

5. Toshiba 55UX600U 55-Inch 1080p 120 Hz LED HDTV with Net TV (Black Gloss)

  • 50 inches designed to eliminate glare from other sources

  • Has a 600 Hz refresh rate and a .001 ms response time

  • Flash drives and cameras connect for slideshows

  • Expected to be a featured item in 2011

6. Samsung PN50C550 50-Inch 1080p Plasma HDTV (Black)

  • Can view in 2D and 3D modes

  • Supports 3D Blu-Ray and 3D broadcasting

  • Response rate is 1,000 times faster than LCD

  • Receives both digital cable and DTV broadcasters

  • Uses a 6-color processing system

  • Expected to be a featured item in 2011

7. Samsung UN55B7000 55-Inch 1080p 120 Hz LED HDTV

  • Crisp, clear clarity attributed also to a 6-color processing system

  • Great for watching sports and any action-packed broadcasts

  • Has a good surround sound, but external speakers would be better

HDTV technology takes visual entertainment enjoyment to a completely different level. With units that are similar in size and image technology, each TV has specific features that makes each one special and the right buy for the right consumer.
