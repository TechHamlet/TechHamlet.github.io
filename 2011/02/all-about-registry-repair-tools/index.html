All About Registry Repair Tools

Feb 20th, 2011 | Comments

A registry repair tool is a type of software developed to clear your PC’s registry of incorrect or obsolete information to ensure an efficiently functioning system. Early operating systems were not as complex as the modern ones such as Windows XP, Vista and Windows 7 and did not need as much maintenance to keep your PC in perfect, fast working order. Nowadays with more complex systems, you should be scanning your PC at least once a month with a registry repair tool to ensure efficiency.

Today drivers and programs are complicated and if not maintained regularly will cause your PC to slow down, increase errors or freeze. A lot of registry repair tools are available either for sale or free download from the internet. Some repair tools include a scanner for Trojan, spyware and malware. Installing one of these tools means you will not need other software.

Registry repair tools can be bought at an average price of US$ 30.00 or you can download a free program from the internet. Similar in action, tools clean and organize your system registry ensuring fast and efficient performance of your PC. Most tools are compatible with Vista, Windows 7 and XP. If you download a free registry repair program check to see whether it includes ignore keys and bug fixes.

The requirements to install most repair programs are Internet Explorer 5.0 (IE) or above, 10 MB free disk space and 32 MB memory. These programs will repair and clean your registry and most  have  backup and restore features  allowing you to restore your system in case of system failure. They also guard against homepage hijack and browser hijack programs.

Downloading a free registry repair tool will require a certain amount of kb and can be downloaded from the internet. Free registry repair tools are updated regularly and their suppliers ensure that they do not contain spyware or malware. If you want to download a free registry repair tool, check to see how many users have already downloaded the same tool and what their comments on usage are. Ensure that when downloading any free registry repair tool you read the menu carefully and follow all steps to download it successfully.

Some registry repair tools can perform a full scan or can only scan certain areas. Satisfy yourself that the program you download will automatically create a backup once errors are fixed and that this backup can be saved with a filename so that you can choose the file if you want to reinstate it.

A registry repair tool should not only be able to scan, fix and restore, but should also offer an option where you can choose what to exclude and whether or not you want to create a system restore point before you fix errors. Programs should be fast and user friendly and should help in reducing a blue screen and improving the function of your PC. Users’ reviews about free download tools are a good indication of the tool’s effectiveness.
