7 Reasons Why WordPress Is the Best Blogging Platform

Feb 20th, 2011 | Comments

WordPress has quickly emerged as one of the best blogging sites available, in fact making new improvements and offering more to bloggers who choose WordPress over other blogging sites. If you’re seeking to make a professional website in addition to a blog, WordPress is the best blogging platform you can choose.

Here are the top 7 reasons why WordPress is the best blogging platform:

1.) One word- plugins! These are a simple way to customize a blog by adding them to photo resources, SEO content, contact forms, boxes and banners with ads, and so many more options. Better yet, a lot of WordPress plugins don’t cost a thing and new ones are added every day!

2.) The overall performance of WordPress is great. Creating websites can be very overwhelming, but WordPress has changed that. With their easy to use program, it’s extremely simple to make a site. If one should get stuck while creating their WordPress blog, all they need to do is visit one of the many forums, support content, or references that WordPress offers.

3.) Permalinks are everybody’s key to your world. They help you get found, and that’s why WordPress creates an individualized permanent link for each blog you post. Additionally, you can add a Permalink to every page or any post you want.

4.) Ping back! This is a tool that lets search engines know when you have a new blog post. All you have to do is add one of those handy-dandy plugins, adjust personal settings, and you’ll start showing up in more searches.

5.) Pathways Back is an incredible little extra that will help grow your traffic. If you leave a comment on another blog, WordPress will automatically put a link to your blog for everyone viewing the blog to see. So now leaving comments on someone else’s page is actually very useful!

6.) The ability to separate your material into different pages so that you can provide more information is now made easy with WordPress’s Pagination.

7.) All in all, WordPress brings out everyone’s personality and allows it to shine. There are hundreds of themes available that you can choose from, and since you have so much say so over what to feature, you are able to create a personal blog, one that has an Internet presence and that represents the writer behind the blog. The available themes are really flexible to change and can also be customizable. If you decide down the road that you want a different look, you can pick a new theme with the just the click of your mouse!

These are just a few of the many reasons that bloggers tend to gravitate towards WordPress. Different platforms are better for different people, so everyone needs to compare blog sites to see what’s best for them, but know that WordPress is always user friendly and always striving to be better than it already is.
