Update : The Final Version of Linux Mint 12 Is Here! Its Officially Announced!

Nov 26th, 2011 | Comments

At first, the Linux Mint developers predicted that they might be able to release the final version of Mint 12 on the 20th of November. But, they are not sure because they wanted to release when ready! Earlier, we managed to grab the RC before they announced it officially. Now, the final version is also here on their official repositories… But, it’s not yet announced!

Just like the RC, they have started uploading the final ISOs before the announcement. Currently, I found one repository with the new Linux Mint 12. We also found the first RC on that repo.

Before you begin, if you don’t know whats so special about Linux Mint 12, please read this post : Linux Mint Goes for Gnome 3 But Promised to Keep the Old Looks!

When you are ready, head over to the repository and download the iso that best suits your machine :  (Before downloading, check the warning below)

Update 1 : The first mirror we showed have removed the files. (Special thanks goes to wichtounet for pointing it out!) I have added a new mirror which still have the files.

Warning : It looks like they are gradually removing the files from the mirrors! Be careful, they might found a bug or wanted to hide them until the announcement. Be careful when you download them. I have already installed Mint 12 but haven’t faced any problems till now. We’ll let you know as soon as we figure out whats going on…

Update 2 : Even Distrowatch have been updated with the latest Linux Mint 12 : http://distrowatch.com/table.php?distribution=mint  But still no word from the official Mint developers!

Warning : At first, we saw Linux Mint ISOs on their servers. Then, big sites like Softpedia announced that the release is final. Even Distrowatch have updated their site. But now, Mint is taking the ISOs back… Some say that they have found a serious bug and have taken them offline for a respin! So, be careful when you download it! Currently I have Mint 12 in my PC but can’t notice any big bugs. But, to be on the safe side, take this warning seriously! We’ll monitor the issue closely and let you know about any updates!

Update 3 : Mint have just replaced the ISOs with new ones. I think they found some big bugs so they made a respin. The updated images are available in almost all the mirrors :

Warning : Again, be careful! Still they haven’t made an official announcement!

Update 4 : Finally, Clem have officially announced the release of the Final Stable Linux Mint 12 on their blog : http://blog.linuxmint.com/

You can now download it from the official site without any fear : http://www.linuxmint.com/download.php
