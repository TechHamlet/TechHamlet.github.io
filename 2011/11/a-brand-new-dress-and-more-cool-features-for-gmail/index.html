A Brand New Dress and More Cool Features for Gmail!

Nov 2nd, 2011 | Comments

After introducing Google+ to the world, Google started to redesign all of its services to the new format they used for their social network. As a result, many services like Google reader got a brand new look. Now, its time for Gmail to change! Want to see whats new? Want to try out the new dress?

Just after Google+, Gmail introduced two new themes called Preview and Preview (Dense). This is the preview of the new Gmail’s new look. Just now, Google have decided to roll this new dress permanently. It’s not just a theme… There’s more than what you saw in the Preview theme!

The new design, now have the Preview look by default. But if you want, you can always change your look. Now, Gmail have several brand new themes. Some are just color changes. The others are HD themes powered by images of iStockPhoto.

If you communicate with one person with several emails, they will be grouped as conversations and will be showed in one mail. Earlier, when it group the conversations, things are a bit…. “Yucky”

Now, Google have changed these conversations and now they are well-organized and wide enough for you to recognize. It will also add profile pictures so that you can easily identify your friends!

By default, the inbox have a lot of space between mail items. Actually its nice and clearer than before. But, if you need you can change it back to normal or compress it more so that it will display more emails.

When it comes to the left sidebar, now it try its best to keep both the labels and the chat window on the screen by adjusting to the size of your screen. You can always change its size by dragging it up and down.

Earlier, if you want to do an advanced search, on your inbox, you have to go to a separate link. Now, just click the small down arrow in the search box and you have advance options!

Actually, we can’t call this a new theme. It’s a new Gmail! Some cool new features and a brand new look to make things clear and easy for the users. According to Gmail, now everyone can test the new look by clicking on the “Switch to New Look” button at the bottom right corner of your inbox :

So, currently you can try it out and switch back to the old UI if you don’t like the new one. But, I don’t think you can think of a good reason to switch back. I love the new changes and now upgrading all my Gmail accounts to the new dress! What about you? Will you also do the same?
