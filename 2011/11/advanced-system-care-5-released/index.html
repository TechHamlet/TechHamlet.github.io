Advanced System Care 5 Is Ready to Boost Your PC! [Just Released]

Nov 15th, 2011 | Comments

For 6 long years, the developers at IObit have been working hard to help you clean, manage, secure and optimize your PCs with Advanced System Care. I got to know about this product after they released the version 3. For a very long time, they kept improving this same version and finally decided to go for the next step at the beginning of this year! They didn’t stop there… Now, they have already gone for their 5th step!

Two major versions in the same year.. Now that’s a big improvement! According to Cnet, Advanced System Care is one of the most downloaded apps in the world. Not only that, its one of the most popular system utility tools which can do its job right…

I have used a lot of system optimization kits before settling for ASC. Most of them were very heavy on resources. How can they speed-up our systems if they are the ones who takes most of the resources? ASC is not like that… It’s very light on the resources. You’ll never notice its presence.

Since the version 3, we reviewed Advanced System Care a lot of times. IObit did a huge change when they moved from version 3 to 4. A brand new interface and a lot of new features. They completely changed the whole software.

ASC 5 don’t have that much noticeable changes. But if you look carefully, there are some exiting new features in this new release. The first thing I noticed is the registry defrag! Many system utility suits have this feature. ASC also had it but it’s not much effective. It never defragged my registry! But the new version have included the registry defrag to their main functions list and its working. I’m really glad to see that feature finally working in ASC because defragging your registry can also help to boost your machine a lot!

Another new feature is the new Cloud database. From now on, your software will get the latest security definitions and the latest updates to the optimization tools instantly via the IObit cloud. The tool is always up-to date!

Active Boost is another big feature coming in ASC 5. This little feature will sit in the background and watch carefully for your system resources and the inactive processes in your system. Then, it will optimize your resources by intelligently managing the CPU and the RAM. Real time optimization!

Apart from those exiting features, there are also some minor interface changes. According to IObit, they have re-written their source code to better support both the 32bit and 64bit architectures. Also the resource usage of the program have been reduced even more.

If you already have Advanced System Care 4 PRO, you can download ASC 5 and upgrade freely. If you don’t have one, you can always try the free version which also have a lot of features enabled in it. So, try it out now and let us know what you think about it…
