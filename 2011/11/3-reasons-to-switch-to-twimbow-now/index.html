3 Reasons to Switch to Twimbow NOW

Nov 6th, 2011 | Comments

When it comes to the way people use Twitter and social media in general, many users find themselves continuously switching from one tool to another, mostly because some features they love in one service are not available in the other and vice-versa. This happened to me all the time, too, and this is the main reason who led me and my team to start thinking of the social media dashboard Twimbow and working hard to bring it to life.

If you search for “Twimbow” on Twitter or simply Google “twimbow”, you can easily realize how much our users love Twimbow and why. We took over one year of private alfa/beta in order to listen to the community carefully and to make sure we were adding the features that could make Twimbow really different from the other alternatives. Why do you think some blogs called Twimbow the “Apple of social media dashboards”? Twimbow is very different from the others and today I would like to give you three main reasons why people decide to switch to Twimbow and stick with it (there are many more, but we love these ones).

1) Colors

Colors are, without any doubt, a very important element of everyone’s life. Colors help you distinguish things and quickly identify what is important for you among other things. Why not using colors to make your social media experience better? On Twimbow, you can create colored Gmail-like labels to your friends so that you can easily distinguish a person or a group of people among the others. Moreover, you can easily filter them in/out in order to focus on their content only or to mute them.

If this is not enough, you can tweet in color so that other users using Twimbow can see your colored status updates.

2) Monitor

The Twimbow Monitor is something you can’t find elsewhere. Instead of making the user interface more crowded with lots of columns like other services do, we decided to make it easier for our users to keep track of what’s going on, by simply adding keywords, tags, users and lists to the monitor (even complex search keywords including operators like OR, AND and so on). When new occurrences are available, a red indicator will show you the number of new occurrences and, on Google Chrome, you can also activate realtime desktop notifications for any item in the monitor. Moreover, you can “send to monitor” users, lists or keywords from other areas of the Twimbow UI (search column, users profiles, list view).

The Monitor is not invasive during normal usage, but can be expanded so that our users can easily browse through the monitor items.

3) Profile Browser

The profile browser is the quickest way to navigate through your twitter friends’ profiles, discover new users you might decide to start following or, last but not least, organize your friends / followers, label them and so on. Many of our users love it and spend a lot of time browsing their followers or discovering new people to follow. You can open multiple profiles at the same time and easily browse them.

 There are tons of other features that make Twimbow different and the best way to discover them is to sign up and try it. In addition, if you want to make sure to stay on top of the new features we keep adding to the service, subscribe to the Twimbowblog and feel free to leave your comments and feedback. You can also easily find me on twitter so please feel free to say hi :–)
