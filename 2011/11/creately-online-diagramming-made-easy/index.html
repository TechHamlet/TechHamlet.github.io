Creately - Online Diagramming Made Easy

Nov 22nd, 2011 | Comments

Creately is an online diagramming tool with an innovative interface to make diagramming easy. It has custom built features for easy collaboration making it the ideal tool for software teams. Their innovative approach to diagramming got them featured in leading technology websites like TechCrunch and MakeUseOf. Before web based software like Creately most people used Microsoft Visio for their diagramming needs. If you are a Mac user it was mostly OmniGraffle. These software were expensive and there was a learning curve involved when using these software. Below are benefits you will gain when you use Creately to draw diagrams online.

  • Easy access to diagrams – Because it’s a web based application you can access your diagrams from anywhere in the world as long as you have Internet access. This makes it an ideal drawing tool for business users, software architects, project managers etc who are constantly traveling in their jobs.

  • Low cost diagram software – A personal plan for Microsoft Visio costs hundreds of dollars while Creately personal plan comes at only five dollars. Even the team plans are very affordable compared to desktop software.

  • Online collaboration – As mentioned above the ability to collaborate makes it the perfect tool for software teams. No need of emailing diagrams to one another, a team can draw diagrams together now. Each revision is saved so you can quickly revert back to a previous edition as well.

  • Click-able diagrams – Another advantage of being a web based application is you can create click-able diagrams. You can link to another web page from your diagrams or you can even link to another diagram. This is especially useful when you want to break down large diagram into much more simpler sub diagrams.

  • Drag and drop images from the web – If you wanted to use images in your diagrams, you generally have to search for them in the web, save them to your machine and then add them to your diagram. With Creately’s built in image search you just have to search for your diagrams in the sidebar and drag those image to your diagram. You have the ability to easily adjust them to fit your diagram as well. A huge time saver.

  • Ability to draw diagrams quickly – There are hundreds of ready made templates andÂ  to start drawing diagrams quickly and adding shapes is just a matter of dragging and dropping

Mentioned above are what makes Creately a great online diagramming tool. But there is so much more to Creately. It has support for many diagram types including for complex types like Gantt charts. There is a diagramming community where you can share, reuse and vote on diagrams. Creately blog has lots of great information about how to draw diagrams and various other things related to diagramming. With all these resource it is one stop website for all your diagramming needs.
