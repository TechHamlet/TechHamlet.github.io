Picfull Gives a New Meaning to Image Editing [Review]

Nov 2nd, 2011 | Comments

Today image editing has become a common thing. For some people, it’s a part of their life and for many it has become a hobby. With the intention of catering to this massive demand image editing sites and softwares have flooded the Internet.

The next main challenge the users have is ‘How to select a good photo editing site or a software?’. Well I assure you that today after reading this you will get the solution for your long awaited question.

Let me introduce Picfull.com  to you. A highly advanced photo editing site.

Why choose Picfull.com :–

   - Has unique, high quality photo filters that are truly easy to use

  – The selection ranges from vintage filters to bolder modern ones each of which has its own set of parameters that can be tweaked by the user in real time

  – Ability to apply multiple filters to the same photo, allowing for almost endless variations

  –  Mainly IT’S FREE!!!

Well let’s see how to work with Picfull.com 

You’ll just have to follow a 4 step process.

 Step 1:

Click “enter your photo now” to begin the journey!!!

Step 2:

Now you can select the necessary picture to be edited and add it to Picfull.

Step 3:

Picfull offers you a lot of frames and each frame has its own parameters. You can edit your picture by correctly adjusting those values.

Step 4:

Once you think that you are done with your photo, you can share your photo among your friends, Facebook, Twitter or just e-mail it to anyone you like. Well not only just share but also you can download your picture from Picfull and use it as a desktop background even.

Still didn’t try it? Why are you waiting? Try Picfull.com for FREE!!!!
