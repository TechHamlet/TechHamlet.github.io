Google Motion : Send Emails With Your Body Movements!

Apr 1st, 2011 | Comments

Now, the Keyboard and the Mouse both are getting old. Google thinks that its the time to give them a retirement and use our own body as a replacement! For a start, Google have just added support to this brand new input device on their famous email client, Gmail! Now, you can send your emails while dancing around the room!

“No longer will people be required bend to the will of technology. Instead, technology will now bend to our will.”

-Dennis Tooley, Ph.D, California Center for Kinesis and Paralanguage

According to Google, this will be our future where we make the technology to bend to our needs. They say that this new system (Gmail/Motion) will improve our productivity and make mail sending faster. Normally, people get sick because they stare at the computer for a very long time. But, this new system will make us move and give some exercise to our body.

But, there’s one problem in this method… According to the above motion chart, we might have to dance well to write a good email. Don’t you think it’s too much exercise? I think that’s why Google have mentioned that we should clear out the area around us before sending our emails. So, the problem is, will you perform a spiritual dance to send your emails rather than simply using the old ways?

The biggest problem is, today is the April Fools day! If you were a fool who didn’t cared to read this whole article, then you might have already got caught to the big prank!

Last year, Google decided to change their company name on the April Fool’s day. This time, they have decided to introduce a new way of dancing! Pretty cool isn’t it.. Anyone who have already fallen for this prank, say “I’m the fool!”
