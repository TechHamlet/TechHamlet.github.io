5 Things to Look When You Follow Someone on Twitter

Apr 18th, 2011 | Comments

You can always follow a bunch of people on twitter just to get follow backed by them. But if you want to market something out to twitter sphere you must engage with the twitter sphere. That is why following users is very important. Don’t fall into the void of thinking that when you get followed by lot of people, you are successful on twitter. What makes you a success is the influence you make on twitter. To make an influence it’s important to follow users on twitter. There are few things to look out for when following people on twitter because nowadays twitter is flooded with spamy accounts.

Description

Description is one of the most important things to look when it comes to following someone. Scan for their niche and who they are. If it contains too much information about a product and nothing personal that’s a good signal that account is spam. Only if an account is made specifically for a product itself then you can ignore the above tip. Plus you can collect information about the account from the description such as area of interests, niche etc. But sadly I have seen people never bothering even to put a description on their account. This shows how careless they are about their twitter presence.

Twitter stream

Scan through the twitter stream of the account. If you find too many same tweets it a good to assume the account is spamy. You can find more information such as if this person is open to conversation or not by his twitter stream.

Follower/following count

I am not saying not to follow accounts with many followers but do try to follow only the crucial accounts. Because mostly these accounts won’t engage much on twitter. Also check how many people does the user actually follow. Then you’ll even get more information about the person’s twitter presence.

Profile picture

Profile picture is also a good way to find more about the user. If you take my account I usually change my profile picture monthly and my profile pictures consists Anime/Manga characters. It’s also a good way to find interests of users. Also accounts with spamy images are not good to follow too. This is not that successful way to find information about an account but it’s one way to do so.

Link

If the account has a link that tells more about the account. Please do visit it and find more information. It’s a good way to find out to what this user is connected to.

That’s the basics when it comes to following a person on twitter. After following users on twitter make sure to engage them on twitter by tweeting great content, holding conversations and any other creative activity you can think of doing on twitter. Make people feel comfortable with your twitter presence and you are capitalizing twitter very efficiently.
