Now You Can Buy Photoshop for Only $35! (Not Only Photoshop...)

Apr 11th, 2011 | Comments

How much do you have to pay for Adobe Photoshop? Nearly $700! Isn’t that too much? Actually, Photoshop is the best graphic designing tool we have on the planet. It’s really easy to use, have a lot of features and also its really stable than the others. But the biggest problem is, it have a really big price on top of it! That’s why people went in search of free tools like Gimp. That’s why people use pirated copies! But now, things might get a bit cheaper!

Today, Adobe have announced that they are going to offer their products in monthly subscriptions! Now you don’t have to pay the ugly $700 at once. Instead, you can pay for the product as you go. For example, you can pay $49 and buy Adobe Photoshop for a month. If that’s also too much for you, you can subscribe for one year. In here, you can pay $35 per month but you have to continue it for a year!

Not only Photoshop, most of their products have this new feature. If you are a student, if you are going to work with Photoshop for only a small period (a small project) or if you just can’t afford to pay $700, you can now go for the monthly subscription! Cool isn’t it? Lets see if this move can reduce the use of pirated copies of Adobe software. But still, the freeware giants should not give up! We need a free tool for image editing which is better than Photoshop.. Can we do it?
