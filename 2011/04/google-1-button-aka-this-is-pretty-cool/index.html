Google +1 Button Aka This Is Pretty Cool!

Apr 1st, 2011 | Comments

The Google, our Search engine buddy has taken a new innovative approach. They are experimenting on a button that Recommends items to people. In Google own words +1 stands for - “the digital shorthand for “this is pretty cool.””. But on the same plane it’s almost equal to the like button that Facebook inaugurated. But this is huge! Search Engine rankings get affected by this new +1 button when it’s fully kicking in the trade. That is why we should pay attention to it.

To start of… This is what the end result will be.

The tiny little +1 button will be there next to the Title of a Search Result. When you press this button +1 recommendation will go directly to the your Google profile. You can immediately opt out from +1 by clicking again (as same as the like button). You can opt out from recommending even by going to the +1 tab on your Google Profile

What’s so Great about it?

Google is trying to increase the relevance of search Results with the +1 button. It’s like combining Digg with Google Search Results. This will help Users to find information recommended by their Social Contacts on top of the Search Results. Now it’s the time for SEO professionals to rethink this new Strategy of Google. Most probably we will have to find new paths to get the most out of this new feature of Google. And it’s too bad for the copycats and the Content Farms that are spreading on the Web.

So how do I participate to this experiment?

Yeah… This is an experiment at the moment. So anyone can join in. It’s only available to Google.com too. So beware of that you won’t see the +1 button on your local search results provided by Google. Just visit here and Sign up for their experiment to give it a try.

I am not that positive about this with the like button of Facebook. Google is said to incorporate this with the AdWords too. But if Google’s Search results are downgrading like it’s now because of all the Content Farm problems. People will gradually move to Bookmarking sites to find Good Quality Content. For an Example it’s easy to find good tutorials by going to a Bookmarking site rather than the Google Search Results.

With that said we can continue expect new functionalities of Google and this is another Awesome feature Google brought up. Google Motion Technology for Gmail. Check it out too.
