Happy New Year! සුභ අළුත් අවුරුද්දක් වේවා! (Get Ready for Give Aways!)

Apr 14th, 2011 | Comments

Every year, on the Month of April, we celebrate the Sinhala and Hindu new year in an auspicious time. According to astrology, today the Sun moves from the Meena Rashiya (House of Pisces) to the Mesha Rashiya (House of Aries) in the celestial sphere. Thats when we celebrate our new year. So, today precisely at 01.01 pm (Sri Lankan time) we will leave our past behind us and step into the new year! So, the celebrations begin!

Last year, we celebrated the new year by giving Tech Hamlet a brand new look. But this time, we have something big to offer.

A brand New Server

Last few weeks, we started to move our site into a brand new Virtual Private Server. After testing for a long time, we are glad to announce that Tech Hamlet is now hosted on a VPS provided by KnownHost. This new VPS allowed us to configure our server in the way we want and make it suitable for our blog. It have also given TH more space to stretch its arms and legs in the way it want!

A new partner

You might have noticed that Tech Hamlet have published a lot of guest posts lately. After our contest, we managed to attract a lot of authors. But, posts made by our friend Dulitha Wijewantha Wijewantha are not going to be guest posts anymore! Now, he is an official author of Tech Hamlet! A young Sri Lankan who have programed himself with Java (A pro Java programmer). So, what do you think? you like his posts?

A wave of give aways!!!

Now, here’s the biggest excitement! Remember how we organized a wave of give aways on January? Well, we are going to do it again to celebrate our new year! According to our traditions, for the new year, we always share gifts with our friends and even with our foes! So, Tech Hamlet is going to give away a bundle of gifts for our readers! The first give away will start today and the wave will last for several weeks. So, subscribe to us and get ready for the gifts!

Last year we celebrated our new year with a little amount of readers, with a small hosting plan, with a design made by a third party and with no gifts. But now, things have changed. Now Tech Hamlet have its own VPS, have a huge amount of readers coming to our site everyday, have our own design carved with Thesis and also have a lot of gifts to give away for the new year! So, what do you think? Isn’t this a huge improvements? What do you think we will do next time? Trust me.. Next time, things will get much bigger!

So, Wish you a prosperous and a peaceful Happy New Year! May all your good wishes come true in this new year!
