Benefits of Using Drupal CMS for Your Website

Dec 11th, 2011 | Comments

Drupal is an open source CMS system crammed with enormous advantages and advanced features. It helps the user to create and edit the content of the website easily. One does not need any technical knowledge of programming to create a website if using the Drupal Content Management System.

If you want to try out the Drupal CMS, which offers you to start a free webpage for starters or a professionally self hosted website for professionals. If you are serious about your site and opted Drupal as your CMS, then there are many reliable Drupal Hosting solutions our there. One must be careful in selecting the best Drupal Hosting solutions by considering the aspects like uptime, 24x7 live support and many more.

Drupal’s well-documented APIs, flexibility and limitless scalability is what is making it more and more popular among the webmasters. Thousands of modules and add-ons will make your website the best of your imagination. Below is a detailed list of the benefits of using the Drupal CMS for your website.

#1. Well organized-

  • There are many different tools in Drupal which help the user to organize, structure and even to re-use the content in an organized way.

  • You can create the friendly path URLs, custom lists, and even categorize the content in your website using Drupal. Drupal Hosting, Drupal Templates, Drupal plugins, and many other exciting features makes it a powerful CMS.

#2. Content management-

  • The easy to use interface of Drupal allows the user to manage the content.

  • You can add videos, polls, podcasts, and other features which help to make your content creative and unique.

#3. Administrative power-

  • Drupal content management system provides the real administrative power to control your website.

  • You get a user id and a password to log in to the control panel of your website. The separate Drupal Hosting importance on most of the Top web-hosting sites explains you the standard.

  • If you want a group of people to work on your website, you can add some more users to the admin page.

  • Even after adding the other users to admin page, you can control on who can see, add, edit or publish the content.

  • In all you are the whole-sole administrator of your website.

#4. Designs and display-

  • Drupal has its own various templates that you can use to create your website.

  • You do not need to know any programming to change the layout of your website.

  • Right from the color scheme to the font type and size, you can change each and everything in your website according to your taste and choice.

5. Extensions-

  • Drupal come with various different extension, plug-ins and add-ons for your website. More than 6000 modules are available in Drupal CMS. The capacity of your Drupal Hosting can be managed really easily without any burden.

6. Easy connections-

  • Once you create your website in Drupal CMS you can connect your website to other services and sites across the web.

  • The social networking integration is the best feature of a Drupal website as it allows the user to get connected worldwide through different leading social networking websites.

  • You can also interact with the external file service and media with your drupal website.

7. Multi-lingual feature-

  • Drupal CMS supports many languages and thus you can add a different version of your page in any language.

  • You can link up any of your page with more than one language and links of this page will be added to the main menu automatically.

Drupal CMS is a free, resourceful, flexible and constantly being developed and improved system. Using the Drupal CMS you can improve the performance of your website to a great extent and hence improve your sales considerably.
