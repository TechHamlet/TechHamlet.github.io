A New Era of Cars Begins

Dec 8th, 2011 | Comments

Hi guys. Glad to be back. When someone talks about a car what will be the first thing that comes to your mind? A square shaped box with seats, wheels, shutters, gears etc., right? But twenty years down the line, this image will be another “Past thingy”.

The Mercedes has got into the job. These pictures were taken at the “What’ll be our future” show held at Paris. Toyota, Nissan, Ford and many more companies were present. But the most innovative design and idea was won by the Mercedes. Want to know how and why? See for yourself….

There are so many cool features in this car and almost all the features a normal Merc will have. Named as SCL600. Here we go into SCL600……….

Upup-away doors


Rear View

Well I know what you all are thinking right now… “What is this? This is cool but nothing special there….”

Well guys the coolest feature comes at last.

No Gea, No Whee

Technology behind this:

Well Merc tries to use a “Fly-by-wire” technology in a car. Today there is only one place where this technology is used. That is, in Aircraft and Fighter Jets. This was introduced to both of them to reduce human errors and the intention of Merc is the same. According to statistics one person meets with an accident in every 4 minutes. Mercs intention is to drastically reduce this amount.

This car is equipped with 187 sensors. There are 5 sensors specially dedicated to monitor driver’s physical and mental conditions. So there is no doubt that this CAR should go home with the award right? For those how wanna know how to buy it? Well, this will be out to the market in 2012 and still the company has not stated a price.
