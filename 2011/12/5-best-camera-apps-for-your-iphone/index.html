5 Best Camera Apps for Your iPhone

Dec 30th, 2011 | Comments

Andriod Cam Apps

iPhones are extensively sporting cameras, which give regular handheld digital cameras a sprint for their money. There are apps for pleasure and fun and there are also apps for essential family moments. If you are serious about your photography, then people tend to opt the Digital Camera or DSLR cameras. There are many extensions available for these like best camera strap upgrade option that which helps you to hold the grip of your digital camera with perfection. We cannot extend the camera options for iPhone or any other smartphones.

Here’s a list of top 4 iPhone camera apps, which are considered to be the best.****

 #1. Camera+

Camera+ presents up to six times zoom, a timer, a burst option, a stabilizer, LED flash, and option to utilize both front and rear camera. It offers high quality picture than stock camera app. It also ensures that all pictures taken by you are saved into the app itself and doesn’t automatically save the pictures to camera roll. The option menu allows you to select which pictures you wish to save to camera roll. You can also share your pictures on Facebook, Flickr, and Twitter, email or SMS. It lets you to copy and paste the pictures that you have taken. It costs about $0.99 only.

2. Instagram

Instagram is the most popular camera app for iPhone that has over 10 million users; it is simple and also free to access. It lets the users to take striking photographs with the assistance of tilt shift blur effects and a range of filters. You can share your pictures on Flickr, Facebook, Tumblr, Twitter, Posterous, and Foursquare through this app.

The features of this app include custom designed borders and filters like Earlybird, Amaro, XPro-II, Hudson, Rise, Toaster, Lomo-fi, Brannan, Sutro, Walden, Inkwell, 1977, Nashville, Hefe, Radial and Linear Tilt-Shift blur effects for added depth of field.

3. Hipstamatic

Hipstamatic is an app, which brings back old time feel, look, impulsive beauty and joy of plastic toy cameras of past days. With just a swipe of your finger, you can change film type, flash, and lens. Every combination puts in exclusive touch to your picture.

It offers 3 flash and film types and 4 different lenses as standard and there are 5 other ‘Hipstapaks” accessible for 99 cents, which include an array of combinations, which recreate old toy cameras.

The key features of this app include swap flashes, lenses, and films for over hundreds of special effects.

Using this app you can directly share your prints on Twitter, Flickr, Facebook, and Tumblr. This app is priced at $1.99.

#4. Big Lens

Big Lens is a camera app, which turns your iPhone into a proficient SLR camera. You may take a picture and alter it by setting aperture, adjusting background light, or varying periphery blur levels to create different effects. Using background adjustment and smart focus tools, you can focus on object in foreground and then darken or blur behind it. The features include smart focus system, filter effects, aperture control before and after preview, and instant focus or blur tool.

#5. PicYou

PicYou is an exciting Camera app that unleashes the new power for your iPhone. PicYou is one of the best Photo App that allows you to share your pics with your friends.

With just 1 or 2 clicks you can connect with your Facebook and Twitter accounts and share your pics with your Social Media friends as well.

It provides some stunning options like adding of frames and filters like Albion, Infactuation, Cuttlefish and many more making it one of the must have iPhone App for Photography.

You can share your pictures on Facebook, Dropbox, Picasa, and e-mail. This app costs you $0.99. Not only for iPhone, we are also having Android Camera apps that helps us to enhance our photography activity.
