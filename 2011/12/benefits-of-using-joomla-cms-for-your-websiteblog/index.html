Benefits of Using Joomla CMS for Your Website/Blog

Dec 11th, 2011 | Comments

CMS (Content Management System) is an application which allows you to control, edit or manage your website content. Many of the open sources however are providing the CMS today but Joomla is of the most popular ones because of some of its awesome features. One can develop any kind of website using a Joomla platform, no matter you want to create a simple personal website or a complex commercial website.

Joomla is the best platform to create highly interactive e-commerce websites, online communities, multi- language websites and media portals. Once you install Joomla in your Hosting for your website, you can manage your content easily. All you need is a user id and a password.

Joomla is most trusted CMS for websites, as you can see on their official site they display huge number of Top websites like eBay, Pizza Hut etc. sites are built on their CMS. So with Joomla you can built everything from a blog to e-commerce websites like ebay. For doing all that you are in need of trusted and reliable Joomla Hosting providers like Hostgator, SiteGround etc., you should not take any kind of chances on your hosting.

Even the layman with no HTML knowledge can use it and maintain the website significantly. Below is an illustrative description of benefits of using Joomla CMS.

1. Free-

  • Usually the CMS solutions providing different features are pricey but Joomla is free of cost. Joomla has numerous modules you might need to add in your website and the best part is that all of them are free to use.

  • Joomla has about 4800 add-ons for your website and you can use all of them without paying a single penny.

2. Easy navigation and time saving-

  • Making a website with Joomla CMS is always easy and time saving. All you need to do is the Joomla installation and you are all set to use the easy and wonderful modules and add-ons.  Joomla Hosting option is existing option in most of the Top web-hosting services.

  • You can add, edit or publish the content on Joomla CMS in real time, without even having the technical knowledge of HMTL.

3. Easy installation-

  • Even you are not a tech savvy and don’t know anything about programming you can install the Joomla easily. You just need to follow a few instructions to install it.

  • It is easy to install Joomla on your Hosting as most of the top hosting sites will be having Joomla Hosting option on their cPanels.

4. Multi-language facility-

  • Joomla supports many different languages and thus you can make your website in any language you are comfortable with.

  • Even if you have created your website in English your viewers can switch to any language they want.

  • This feature provides the easy accessibility to your viewers.

5. Regular developments-

  • The webmasters of Joomla project team keep on making the regular and up-to-date changes in the Joomla CMS.

  • After every 15-20 days, Joomla come up with a new development to make your website better.

  • The user can easily upgrade the website to the newer available version. As, a result your website’s as well as Joomla Hosting security aspects will keep on improving regularly.

6. Customer support forums-

  • If you are facing any problem in maintaining your Joomla CMS website, you can submit the report or a bug, or you can even create a thread in the support forum of Joomla.

  • The queries and bugs are soon fixed by the Joomla community.

7. Multi-user authentication-

  • You can create multi-user authentication in your Joomla website. Anybody can login to your website with your permission.

  • So if you have a group of people working for a website, it would be easier with Joomla CMS website.

In a nutshell we can say that Joomla is one of the best choices to create a website with CMS. Many leading e-commerce and informative websites are running successfully on Joomla CMS. Its amazing features have bagged a good reputation and great user base in a short duration.
