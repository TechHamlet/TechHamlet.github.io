How to Write Better Content Using Google Analytics

Dec 20th, 2011 | Comments

In April 2005 when Urchin Software Corporation was attained by Google, a whole new product was introduced which gave a rich insight into detailed statistics of different websites of various countries. The main goal of Google analytics is the production of advertisements, leading the sales, email marketing and across search engines, the productivity of keywords. It’s a widely used website, used by around 49.95% users worldwide. Generally used by marketers and technologists, this website is like an instantaneous idea generator. It is open to anyone who wants to sign up. Lately in April 2011, a new version of Google analytics has been introduced with new multiple versions. It shows you who have used your website, how long they stayed and what they read. With the help of Google analytics you can strengthen your marketing initiatives and create higher and advanced websites.

Content Writing to Online Writing

Content writing has been immensely used by public. Starting from the content of the newspapers it has now approached online writing. Almost every other person is writing online for various purposes starting from recreational to educational intentions. An enhanced content has to be motivating, appealing and inspiring.  After Google, Google analytics is extensively used as it gives one quick idea about what people stipulate. Generally in content, searchers normally look out for conspicuous and stand-out words and sentences. To make a content more interesting, usage of idioms and phrases should be used. It ought to be short, informative and to the point.

What to Search?

While searching material for your content, if one goes through Google analytics you can find more. Through this powerful, easy-to-use and influential website you can find statistics and analyze data and information of the sites and the ads used at present. Content will be authentic if used more. Surveys take more time if you have to. There are multiple features which includes a component called: Google Analytics Tracking for Adobe Flash which contains the functionality of the Google Analytics JavaScript code. If we have to write about social content, there is a feature called “Social Media Metrics Grease monkey Plug-in” which is like a Firefox plug-in. With the usage of small bits of JavaScript it helps you customize the display page. It has links with the social websites as well. It helps you make the analytics data more human. If writing about a particular website, we have to provide relevant content. Their work centers around marketing goods and the services of websites that are promoting or endorsing them. The website provides with the statistics, data and the graphics. If you do not use this site as a tool for your content, it won’t have the information that people aspire for.

Although the essential material for content writing can be searched by a million websites but at the end of the day, the technique of writing and presenting it in an interesting and innovative yet understandable manner is the true content writer’s style. Google analytic is the only website that fulfills the above statement. This website help you in viewing how much audience you have for your contents.
