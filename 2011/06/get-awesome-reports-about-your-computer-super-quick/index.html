Get Awesome Reports About Your Computer Super Quick

Jun 13th, 2011 | Comments

Now today we have our computers and many other digital devices out there and we need some quick infoabout our computer. So what do you do? Check system requirements from My Computer. If you need a full report this might be a good choice. But why worry that much when only you want to do is to know whether cookies are enabled in your Browser? Or when you want to know the screen resolution of your Jambo monitor exactly. There is a site that does that and that is SupportDetails.

It generates a useful page with great information. OS, Screen Resolution, Web Browser version, IP Address, Javascript enabled/disabled, Cookies enabled/disabled, Color Depth. But this not all what SupportDetails can do. It can send the report as a mail to a recipient that requires it. If you want to send a report about your computer for support purposes sending SupportDetails report will help you from gathering data about your computer.

If you observe the report you can see that it’s clear, simple and is useful. This is kind of report that we require. But most of the information presented are useful to Web Browser details. Web App developers can use these reports more than others because of that. But anyway the world is going to Web 2.o web base apps so these types of reports are better.

More than that you can even export the information as a PDF or CSV which will be helpful for your future use.
