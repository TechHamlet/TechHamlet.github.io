LulzSec Declares War! The World War III Has Begun!

Jun 21st, 2011 | Comments

LulzSec (Lulz Security) is the group who attacked several well-known websites including Sony, PBS, Fox and even CIA. Earlier, they did this for fun. Sometimes they published all the personal information including usernames and passwords they obtained by hacking these sites. Sometimes, they just hacked and informed the owner about their vulnerabilities so that they can patch them. Some of these sites offered to pay them but they refused it because they were hacking just for fun. But yesterday, they changed their target and declared war against all the governments, banks and large organizations in the world!

At first, they tried to show us how the big co-operations like Sony ignored their users’ security. They showed us how people can easily hack our accounts. Many people might think that they are an evil force because they posted all our hacked information in the public. But, their argument is, if they didn’t post them, we don’t know that we are hacked. There are millions of hackers around the world who hack into these accounts secretly and use the information for their own use. But, many people have used the information LulzSec posted publicly to attack those innocent users. LulzSec have enjoyed this! Now this is something unacceptable!

That’s the past. Yesterday, they joined with more hacker groups like Anonymous and declared war! They made this announcement through their Twitter account as the 1000th tweet :

Salutations Lulz Lizards,

As we’re aware, the government and whitehat security terrorists across the world continue to dominate and control our Internet ocean. Sitting pretty on cargo bays full of corrupt booty, they think it’s acceptable to condition and enslave all vessels in sight. Our Lulz Lizard battle fleet is now declaring immediate and unremitting war on the freedom-snatching moderators of 2011.

Welcome to Operation Anti-Security (#AntiSec) – we encourage any vessel, large or small, to open fire on any government or agency that crosses their path. We fully endorse the flaunting of the word “AntiSec” on any government website defacement or physical graffiti art. We encourage you to spread the word of AntiSec far and wide, for it will be remembered. To increase efforts, we are now teaming up with the Anonymous collective and all affiliated battleships.

Whether you’re sailing with us or against us, whether you hold past grudges or a burning desire to sink our lone ship, we invite you to join the rebellion. Together we can defend ourselves so that our privacy is not overrun by profiteering gluttons. Your hat can be white, gray or black, your skin and race are not important. If you’re aware of the corruption, expose it now, in the name of Anti-Security.

Top priority is to steal and leak any classified government information, including email spools and documentation. Prime targets are banks and other high-ranking establishments. If they try to censor our progress, we will obliterate the censor with cannonfire anointed with lizard blood.

It’s now or never. Come aboard, we’re expecting you…

History begins today.

Lulz Security,

http://LulzSecurity.com/

Support: http://www.mithral.com/~beberg/manifesto.html

Support: http://www.youtube.com/user/thejuicemedia

Support: http://wikileaks.ch/

Support: http://anonyops.com/

–Operation Anti-Security (pastebin.com)

Their main target is to reveal how the governments and the big co-operations misuse our information. They want to reveal their corruption. They have also started to follow WikiLeaks which is the biggest whistle blowing site on the planet. Some people say that CIA and FBI have been buying our data from Google and Facebook. If LulzSec can find the truth about these rumors and ensure that non of them can track us, then people might treat them as gods!

Earlier they fought the World War with guns, bombs, fighters and battleships. But now its the era of information technology. Now they are going to fight the World War III in the Internet. The main weapon they are going to use is “DDoS” (Denial of Service Attack). A deadly weapon which can take down many websites around the world!

Now what do you think? Who is the Evil Force in this War? The governments or the LulzSec? The biggest question is, who is the next target?
