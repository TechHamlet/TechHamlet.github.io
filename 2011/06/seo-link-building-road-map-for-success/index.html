SEO Link Building Road Map for Success

Jun 13th, 2011 | Comments

Success in search engine optimization (SEO) link building depends entirely on understanding the process as “inherently iterative,” says John Tawadros, a SEO specialist at iProspect. In other words it is a process, not a project nor an end-in-itself.

Strategies for SEO Linkages Success

Experts’ in the field advice that every search engine optimization (SEO) strategy itself shall be geared for results by implementing best practices and staying on top of emerging SEO optimization tactics.  Some basics to be taken into account are namely – search engine friendly site design and architecture. Ensure that the fundamentals of keyword research and content development are in place. To register one’s site with appropriate online directories. Capture Commerce, a team of Internet marketing professionals recommend that your link building for SEO success shall rests on four areas. These are – establishing link exchange relationships with other quality and on-topic websites. Publishing press releases and articles online (on relevant news sites). Also developing a link worthy resource or tool that becomes “viral” on the Web. Choosing carefully which social media sites are useful for link building; because others are not.

Maximizing SEO Linkages

As part of maximizing your SEO link building, it is advisable to check your link popularity with the free tool available online at http://www.marketleap.com/publinkpop/. The site also allows you to compare your link popularity to up to two competitors, advices denning e-solutions, specialists web site design and Internet marketers. They also advice that SEO enthusiasts shall make a list of related businesses with whom they have a relationship. These shall include professional organizations, vendors and suppliers that may agree to place a link of your site on theirs. Each new link to your site increases the likelihood of both the search engines’ spiders running across your site as well as searchers looking for services or products like yours.

SEO Linkages Best Practices

According to the Google SEO Guide, using Google’s Webmaster Tools can help you identify issues that, if addressed, can help your site perform better in search results although it won’t help your website gets preferential treatment. The tool helps webmasters better control how Google interacts with their websites and get useful information from Google about their site. Users will inadvertently type in the wrong URL or follow a broken link – Google advises SEO specialists to make use of custom 404 page that kindly guides users back to a working page on your site. This can greatly improve a user’s experience. And your 404 page should probably have a link back to your root page that could also provide links to popular or related content on your site. Google provides a 404 widget that you can embed in your 404 page to automatically populate it with many useful features. Matt Brooks, SEO specialist contends that you must syndicate your RSS feed, not your article content. Brooks asserts that keeping the content on your site helps you to ‘ping’ other services like technorati, twitter, and facebook.
