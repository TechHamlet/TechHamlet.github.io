4 Places Where You Can Write, Publish and Share

May 23rd, 2011 | Comments

If you want to share some words with your friends…. Where will you go? Facebook? If you share it on Facebook, only your friends in Facebook can read it! What about your Twitter friends? Your Reddit friends? Or others who don’t use any social networks? Then we need something which is not related to any social network! Something which is free and easy to use! Is this possible? Yes it is!

One day I tried to get some help on the Wordpress forums. I tried to paste some htaccess codes but Wordpress removed them and asked me to use pastebin and share the URL! That’s how I discovered these write and share services. It’s very simple… You write something (any junk). After you are done, they’ll give you a simple link which you can share with anyone! You can Tweet the link, you can share the link on Facebook, you can mail the link, etc. Some services will even let you share your content privately! So, you don’t have to worry about strangers having a look at your text! Today, we are going to look at 4 top services in this industry :

Pastebin.com

This one of the oldest and the well-known write and share services we have on the net. Unlike most of the others, Pastebin have a lot of options. If you sign up for Pastebin, you can edit or delete what ever you write. But still you can write as a guest (but you won’t  be able to make any changes!). Pastebin will also allow you to highlight your code according to the correct syntax! (It supports syntaxes of many programming languages). One of the biggest features I saw in Pastebin is the ability to you set your paste to expire after a certain time. Not only that, you can also make your paste public and private . If you make the paste public, it will be openly published on the pastebin website giving you many readers! The readers can embed your paste on their sites or can even download them as a text file. Very powerful, have a lot of options and also have more control over your content!

Pen.io

The name is simple, the design is simple, and everything is clean and simple when it comes to Pen.io. First you have to create your own Pen.io page by giving it a name and a password. Then you can start writing! You can also edit your page if you can still remember the password. Unlike in pastebin, the URL you have to share in Pen.io is very short and memorable. But there’s no way to make them private! The top pages will always be accessible to everyone through pen.io/top !

Freedomshare.eu

Like Pastebin, Freedomshare also have some cool features. It also have the option to make your posts private so that only the people with the URL can access your content. Also you can make your posts editable by adding a password. Unlike the others, Freedomshare cares more about security. So, you have to fill a Captcha before you can publish anything! I hate Captcha! The Biggest feature I saw here is the comments! You can enable this feature to let your readers comment on your post! Cool isn’t it?

Tidypub.org

This is the simplest and the fastest method to share your writings. You only have to write your content and hit “Publish”. Then you can take the URL and start sharing! According to Tidypub, everything you share on it will be private. But, you can’t edit them in the future! It will be good for people who are in a hurry and need a simple and an easy service to get their job done!

Actually, I can’t say which one is the best. Because each of the above services have their own unique uses. You just have to determine your needs and pick the correct one! So, what did you pick?
