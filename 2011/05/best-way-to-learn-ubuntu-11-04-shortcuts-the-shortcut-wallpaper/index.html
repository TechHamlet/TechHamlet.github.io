Best Way to Learn Ubuntu 11.04 Shortcuts : The Shortcut Wallpaper

May 1st, 2011 | Comments

Recently, Canonical released their latest version of Ubuntu, Ubuntu 11.04. This new release comes with a brand new shell built by the Ubuntu team which is called Unity. Unity have changed everything… It have changed the regular desktop and have made everything simple and minimal. So, to take the maximum out of Unity, we might have to learn how to use its shortcuts!

The hard way is to go through the whole documentation and memorize the shortcuts. This will be a bit difficult because Ubuntu have added a lot of shortcuts. Then, lets go for the easy way. Recently, LifeHacker have discovered a cool wallpaper with Unity shortcuts! This is the easiest way for you to learn Unity’s shortcuts. When you need something, just minimize your work and have a look at the wallpaper to find the correct shortcut! This is what I call “learn by doing”! So, what do you think?
