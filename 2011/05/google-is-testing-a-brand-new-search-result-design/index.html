Google Is Testing a Brand New Search Result Design!

May 7th, 2011 | Comments

Google have started testing again! Recently, many people have spotted a brand new search result page on Google. A more lighter, cleaner and a specious page. As the world’s number one search engine, and due to the rapid change in Technology, Google have decided to redesign their search results to satisfy their user’s needs. So, recently, they have started the testing their new features with several lucky users!

Ian Chan have just spotted a cool change in his Google search results and decided to share the screenshots through TwitPic :

The new design looks to be more clean isn’t it? Why is that? Google have added a lot of space between individual search results and have added a dotted line to separate them. Also the space between the title and the description have been increased.

Not only that, now the colors are also very soft for the eyes. Instead of the normal blue color they used for the titles, they have added a light blue color and a grey color to the descriptions (instead of pitch black).

Also look at the site links in the above image… See the icons? I think they have also started to show favicons and Gravatars in their search results. Cool isn’t it?

Google have made a lot of changes to their site in the past few months… A new home page, a new toolbar, a new sidebar, a new logo, a new image search, new instant search and now, they are focusing on the search results! So, what will they change next? Is there anything left for them to change? What do you think about this new change? Want to know my idea? Actually,I think it’s really great! Now everything seems to be more clean and clear!

-Special thanks goes to Mr Image by Mr Ian Chan for the screenshots!
