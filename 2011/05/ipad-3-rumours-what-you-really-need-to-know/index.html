iPad 3 Rumours: What You Really Need to Know

May 7th, 2011 | Comments

Now that the Ipad2 hit the market and millions of copies were sold , we now all want to know when the iPad3 will hit the market?  In late april,  Apple said that it has no plans to release another iPad in 2011 because the iPad2 sold very well. They also want to know how the market plays out and make a decision on the iPad 3 in 2012. Experts don’t know if Apple is making a good decision because with the raise of the number of tablets hitting store shelves in the past months, they would think it might be a good idea for Apple to release the iPad3 at the end of the current year. An iPad’s 3 release might be a possibility in 2011 but it’s very unlikely.

But one of the question is why Apple didn’t produce more iPads based on holiday sales? They have replied by saying that a product transition is always difficult and as well as rolling out a new iPad would be very hard in time for the holidays. But we have to use our logic because Apple can’t rush things up, imagine if they launch two big products in a single month? They’d have to sell the iPhone 5 as well as an iPad 3.  They can’t make launches each month and convince people that the iPad 3 is worth owning so soon after the iPad 2.A product transition is indeed difficult and doing different launches in less then six months would be a nightmare. Don’t forget the reaction they’d get from the people who are still waiting for their iPad 2 and get it just in time to see the iPad 3 roll out.You also have to consider the pricings of the tablets, it’s not everyone that can pay more then 500$ for an iPad tablet.  As consumers with a limited income, we have to consider purchase decisions. I will admi that an iPad once every year or every other year isn’t really a huge deal but the problem is that if you start loading consumers with too many choices (2 ipads and an iPhone within one calendar year) you might have problems. Either way, this sounds like pretty strong evidence that the iPad 3 won’t launch in 2011.
