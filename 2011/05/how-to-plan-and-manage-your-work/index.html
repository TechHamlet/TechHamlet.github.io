How to Plan and Manage Your Work

May 6th, 2011 | Comments

You maybe just an “average Joe”, awesome/cool or even very popular! No matter who you are and what your profession is, I personally think… Everyone should have to-do lists! Why?

It really makes our lives easy! We won’t forget anything (to-be-done). And there are two good reasons for us, to maintain a to-do list:

Punctuality – less stress and Personal satisfaction

Punctuality – less stress It’s about doing our work on time, but it’s more about doing particular things at the right time. Now, we have to decide “when” to do “what”

I can recommend you two efficient ways of deciding “when” to do “what”

  1. You surely have an idea of how your day is going to be, at least a brief imagination. So, prepare your to-do list every night – for the following day, in an orderly manner; you start from the top of your list and end at the bottom (completing one-by-one in order).

  2. Take a look at the diagram below

You can work according to this (priority-wise), if you like to, but I myself don’t use it because it seems too complicated. “However, practicing this will teach you how to plan your things well,” my lecturer said! True.

Personal satisfaction This is obvious; when you do something correctly, you get satisfied with your work and that makes you happy, which actually, is the main concern. Tip: don’t rush, take time to complete your work.

Ways of “to-do” listing

  1. You can use a notebook (suggestion: a page a day)

  2. Drafted messages on your mobile phone (where to-dos don’t have to be in order)

  3. Microsoft Word (Google Doc.) / http://todoist.com – if you have access to a computer …with an internet connection
