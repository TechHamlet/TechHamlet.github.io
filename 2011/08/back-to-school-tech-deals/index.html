Back to School Tech Deals

Aug 3rd, 2011 | Comments

It’s that time of year again, where we all go out in a mad rush to find everything on that back to school shopping list. Everywhere you look there are easy to find deals at your local office supply store for discounts on wide ruled paper and rulers, but it can be difficult at times to find discounts on those big ticket items you need like an iPad or that expensive software for your computer.

Take advantage of your student status and get some of the best deals around on technology you can use daily in your student life.

Free Xbox 360

Ever wanted a free XBOX 360…for school of course? Then make sure a new PC is on your back to school list. Students that purchase a select Windows 7 PC for a minimum purchase price of $699+ from the Microsoft Store before September 4, 2011 will receive a free XBOX 360 4GB model. You must have a valid and verifiable “.edu” email address to receive the promo code for the deal via email.

More details here:  Free XBOX 360

Free Study App for Mac, iPhone, & iPod touch

SimpleLeap Software is giving away their popular study app, Cram for the Mac and iPhone for free on August 2, 2011. Cram for Mac usually retails for $24.99 and Cram for iPhone usually retails for $3.99.

You must purchase the new iPhone/iPod touch game title Goop from Fine & Dandy Games for $0.99 on August 2, 2011 to receive the deal. SimpleLeap requires you to send the receipt of your Goop purchase to receive either the Mac or the iPhone version of the study software for free.

More details here: Free Study App

Get $100 iTunes Giftcard

Apple is offering a free $100 Back to School Card to use on the Mac App Store, the App Store, the iTunes Store, and the iBookstore.

To receive the $100 iTunes Gift card you must buy a new qualifying Mac with Apple education pricing from June 16, 2011, through September 20, 2011.

Also, keep in mind that as a student you receive a small discount with the education pricing Apple offers on products like the Macs. Since your gift card is sent automatically, you are getting a two-part deal – a discount on the Mac and $100 gift card. Great deal!

More details here: Apple Back to School Promo

Double Cash Back

If you don’t know about eBates, it’s time to get to familiar with this awesome site. Most of your purchases online can actually earn your a bit of cash back if you use this site to make your purchase. The best part about eBates is their special Back to School Double Cash Back program. They’ve teamed up with particular retails like HP, Dell, and Lenovo just to name a few, to offer some serious cash back to students heading back to school. Take advantage of these deals now, because they aren’t going to be around for long.

More Details here: Back to School Double Cash Back

Gadget Theft Protection

You wouldn’t think that you would necessarily need theft protection on your back to school list, but sadly there is an insane amount of gadget theft in schools and on campuses.

The folks over at GadgetTrak have a deal offering students 30% on theft recovery software for  laptops (Windows & Mac) and smartphones (iPhone/iPad, Android, & Blackberry). You install the GadgetTrak app on your device and if it gets stolen you can track its location and get it recovered. Simply use the coupon code BACKTOSCHOOL through September 15th on Gadget Trak’s online store to get your 30% savings.

More details here: 30% Gadget Theft Protection

****Of course there are many more deals like these available all over the Internet on sites like Offers.com or eBates.com.  Just check in everyday to get the best back to school deals on these sites.
