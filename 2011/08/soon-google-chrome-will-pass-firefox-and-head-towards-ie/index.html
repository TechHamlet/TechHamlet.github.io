Soon Google Chrome Will Pass Firefox and Head Towards IE

Aug 9th, 2011 | Comments

Few years ago, Firefox and Internet Explorer were the top players in the browser world. Soon, many other players like Opera and Safari tried to enter this war. Then came Google Chrome. It came as the most simplest browser and it managed to make a huge revolution in the browser world! Chrome redesigned the whole web browser and managed to make other developers run for new releases. Now, it’s aiming at the gold medal!

Ever since Google released Chrome, it became very popular in a very short time period. Still its in the third place but it’s gaining on the others really fast. At the beginning of this year, we saw how Firefox beat IE and became the number one browser in Europe (But now, IE have gained a small bit of popularity than Firefox). But, after digging in to the stats, we got to know that the main actor which helped Firefox to gain on IE is Chrome. Google Chrome kept eating IE’s popularity while Firefox managed to stand still.

Now, if we come to current worldwide stats, you’ll see that IE keeps falling dramatically. The number one falling browser… But still its at the top. When it comes to Firefox, It have managed to keep a straight line while others fight for the first place. But now, it have also started to drop little by little.

What about Google Chrome? If you see the stats I attached below, you’ll see clearly how Chrome’s usage have been rising like a rocket! It’s the fastest growing browser in the market! If it go like this, soon it will pass Firefox and become the number 2 browser in the world.  After that, I don’t think Chrome have to try harder to take down IE. Its weaker than Firefox. So, it will fall easily for Google Chrome! So, if im correct, at the end of this year, Chrome will become the number 1 browser in the world! What do you think?
