Google Launches Google Wallet- a Smart Way to Do Shopping

Sep 21st, 2011 | Comments

Hi guys. I’m here to unwell yet another cool feature or rather an application launched recently by the Google Company. The google wallet. Have you ever being so frustrated of loosing your wallet or forgetting it at a store? Well for those who constantly forgets google wallet is a smart way to do shopping.

As the first step this cool feature only works with Citi-Mastercards and the Google Prepaid Card. Visa and Google are still under negotiations.

Hopefully this opportunity will be available to visa card holders too. So don’t give up.

Google wallet works with Nexus S 4G and with any of the thousands of PayPass merchants. Which means there is another surprise for google wallet users. You can simply wave off your phone whether you are in a taxi cab or at Wallmart.

Today hardly anyone can be found without a mobile phone. I have sometimes forgotten to take my wallet or the watch but not my mobile. And I have encountered plenty of situations where I search the wallet but ended up telling the cashier “to keep these goods aside I’ll pick up my wallet and come” which is sometimes humiliating.

I find it blessed that google came up with such an idea.If you haven’t used a NFC or a Near Field Communication device before well…. you might take sometime to get used to this. But google has made this less complicated. It only functions when the screen is unlocked. It automatically switches to locked mode after sometime so that unnecessary money transactions will not be done.

I know the next question which comes to your mind when I tell this is “What if someone steels this?”. Well the answer for that would be, don’t worry. Google has developed a back-caging system so that when you report that you have lost your mobile or the tablet google quickly disables the system and what’s more interesting is that since google tracks all the transactions it’ll be very easy to know the location of the thief. May be they’ll have to come up with a smarter idea in this regard :)

Google demonstrates this feature in a Macy’s POS terminal.

Privacy And Security

Well there’s almost nothing to worry about privacy. GOOGLE WILL ONLY track the location and the price but NOT THE NAMES and DETAILS of the products.

Also it’s more secure than your normal wallet since you can put a pin to your google wallet.

Well trust me guys you might wanna check this out. Believe me it’s worth it!!!

http://www.google.com/wallet/
