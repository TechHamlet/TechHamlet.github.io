A Social Meetup in Sri Lanka : Refresh Colombo

Sep 22nd, 2011 | Comments

As it’s mentioned on their website, Refresh Colombo is a community of web and technology enthusiasts/professionals who come together on a monthly basis to share ideas and meet like ‘minded’ individuals. These events are open to anyone, who wants to attend them; hackers, bloggers, coders, computer experts, journalists, techies, entrepreneurs and venture capitalists are all welcome – you can even take your grandma.

Refresh Colombo Intro from Hamid Afzal on Vimeo.

What is expected of  ’Refresh Colombo’ is to uplift professionalism and entrepreneurial skills of the Sri Lankan tech community, to reach greater heights. The monthly gatherings are also a great opportunity to build fruitful and professional networks. This amazing get-together happens on the 3rd/4th week (on Thursdays at 5.30 pm – 7.30 pm) of every month, at the Royal College Union Skills Centre: Rajakeeya Mawatha, Colombo-07, Sri Lanka. View the agenda for September’s meetup if you’re interested in making it there, meeting new people, learning new things and rocking the show.

Hey, wait! Get connected with Refresh Colombo via Facebook  and Twitter You can tweet with  the (#RefreshColombo) hashtag, and confirm your participation on Facebook.
