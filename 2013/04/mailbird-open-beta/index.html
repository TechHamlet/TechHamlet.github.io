Mailbird : The Better Mail Client for Windows Is Now Open for Everyone

Apr 2nd, 2013 | Comments

Mailbird

Remember the cool email client we reviewed on January? At that time, people were unable to try it because it was in closed beta. But now, Mailbird is open for anyone to download and try! So, what are you waiting for?

What most of our email clients lack is a good appealing interface, speed and ease of use. There are many email clients available for Windows but they are ugly or full of unwanted features which make them hard to use and painfully slow. Because of this, Andrea and her team thought of creating a brand new mail app which can solve all these issues.

Mailbird have a very elegant look. You’ll start to love it on first sight! It’s also really fast. According to Mailbird, it’s the fastest email client ever. When it come to usability… Mailbird will get full marks. It’s simplicity makes it really easy to use. After following the initial tutorial, every one should be able to find their way around the app.

For a full overview of the app, check my previous review : Mailbird : A More Elegant, Fast and a Better Mail Client for Windows. Still the app is in Beta! You might feel some features are missing and you will face some bugs, but the Mailbird team will fix them fast! Now we are waiting for the final release!
