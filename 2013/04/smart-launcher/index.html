Smart Launcher : A Simple, Fast and a Unique Launcher for Android

Apr 17th, 2013 | Comments

Most of us choose custom launchers for our Android devices rather than the one shipped by default. There are many launchers for us to choose. Many have the same layout and work in the same way. The difference is with features and customizability. But the Smart Launcher is completely different!

A normal launcher will have several home screens. You can add widgets and customize your home screens. If you need to select an app, you have to go to the app drawer which will open in front of the home screen or you can use a shortcut placed in the home screen. But the Smart Launcher acts in a different way. There are no multiple home screens and no widgets (Widgets are available only in the PRO version). The home screen have the clock and some shortcuts nicely organized in a circle.

The home screen of the Smart Launcher :

Smart Launcher Home

Remember the Ubuntu phone? It have a bar in the left side which will have the shortcuts. It will appear only if you swipe from the left edge towards the center. Smart Launcher also have a similar feature. It doesn’t have shortcuts. The left bar has several categories which it have grouped your apps. According to the developers this is the only launcher which can group your apps according to their functionalities.

You can also access the app drawer by selecting the 6 squares in the bottom left corner. If you drag your finger across the categories, it will move really fast. The launcher is really fast!

The app drawer of Smart Launcher :

Smart Launcher App Drawer

According to the developers, the Smart Launcher is very light on memory. It is very lighter than most of the other launchers. But it’s not that light as the apex launcher. A tiny difference which you will never notice.

The launcher is free but you can upgrade to the PRO version for $3.49. The PRO version will allow you to add widgets to the home screen. But widgets can make things slow. They consume more memory. I don’t know about you but I can live without any widgets!

So, what do you think? The launcher is completely different and have attracted the attention of many users. Give Smart Launcher a try and feel the difference!
