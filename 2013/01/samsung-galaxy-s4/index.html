Samsung Galaxy S4 : The Best New Future Phone

Jan 19th, 2013 | Comments

Hi, I am Samsung Galaxy S IV. I belong to the fourth generation of Samsung’s Galaxy S family. I was born in the minds of great engineers. I come from South Korea. My ancestors also came from the same place. I belong to a very big family. A family, Samsung which is not only a name now rather is more than that. It’s a brand image of Success, Innovations & Ideas.

Many Web blogs and other Gadget Experts are predicting my date or birth, some have also posted my images and my specs before my arrival.

This shows all your eagerness and enthusiasm towards my arrival. I know, many of you are waiting to make me your next Smartphone. But what is so special in me? What is the key feature in me, which is turning people around the globe mad about me?

I will tell you what i am, to which extent i can go. I will tell you, what I am!! As you already know, i come from the South Korean manufacturer Samsung. I am the Next Generation of Samsung’s Galaxy S Series. I am a superfast Smartphone which has all capabilities, that make you enable to meet tomorrow.

I am an example of innovation which Sports a 5 inch Super AMOLED Touch Screen producing 16M colours at 1080 x 1920 px resolution. Beneath, my heart is powered by 1.8GHz Quad Core Processor, Chipset: Exynos 5450 Quad coupled with 2GB RAM. My heart runs on system called Android 4.2 Jelly Bean OS.

For my eyes, I have a 13 Megapixels Autofocus Rear Camera, with LED flash which is capable of Recording HD video and image recording, geo-tagging, touch focus, face and smile detection, image stabilization. I also carry a 2 megapixels front camera for having an eye on you.

I have a great 16GB internal memory, and you can insert a microSD card of 64GB in my brain to increase my brain’s memory capacity.

On Connectivity fronts, i support 3G, Wi-Fi, GPS with A-GPS, GLONASS, DLNA, NFC, Bluetooth v4.0 with A2DP, EDR, Wi-Fi hotspot & microUSB.

My features’ list also carry S-Voice natural language commands and dictation, Smart Stay eye tracking, Document viewer, Dropbox, TV-out, SNS integration and many more.

So, I informed you all about myself. Now, you can have many reasons to make me your next Smartphone. I am a future, which is happening in present. I have all the capabilities to merge your tomorrow and today.
