Happy New Year!, Happy Anniversary and a Gadget Giveaway!

Jan 1st, 2013 | Comments

Happy New Year

We have just survived the Mayan apocalypse and now stepping into the next year! Today is the 1st of January… It’s the start of a new year and the day we started the TechHamlet project! Just as usual, we are organizing some big events for our readers…

Most of the people feared that 2012 will be the end. But already, it have been proved wrong. The world didn’t end! We are still alive! So, its time to move on…

Actually, 2012 was a bit difficult year for us. We opened a new website and added the best design for Tech Hamlet. But, we had issues keeping it always updated in the middle of the year. At the end, we managed to bring it back up. In 2013, things are going to change…

Several years ago, to celebrate our anniversary, we started to giveaway free software! A lot of big software vendors like IObit, Bitdefender, IDM, TuneUp, iolo, Zemana, Ashampoo offered free keys for our readers. We gave away free software for several years and of-course it will continue this year also…

But, this year… we are going to take the giveaways to the next level! We are going to start giving away free gadgets! First, it’s going to be a popular Android Tablet! Try to guess what it is. Its hidden in the above picture!

Finally, happy new year to everyone and try to correct your mistakes and start a new life in 2013! We’ll start the giveaway in the next few hours! So, get ready!
