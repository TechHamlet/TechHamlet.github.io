Structuring and Using Social Media Correctly to Promote Your Website

Mar 16th, 2013 | Comments

Image Source : socialable.co.uk

Over time, social media has developed into a vital component of Internet marketing. The effectiveness of social media is unrivalled; it enables you to continually build relationships with prospective customers on the web. Your Twitter or Facebook feed could very well turn into a brand by itself, allowing you the capability to quickly share information and special offers with all your social media followers.

Your Social Media Profile

To begin with, you need your website’s homepage to be not only eye-catching, but also interesting. To make it appealing to your visitors make sure to use easy-to-understand information, quality images, and provide a link to your website.

Focus on Your audience

Once you have your goal, define your target audience. Sometimes it is trickier at first because it may not be clear. You may have to be somewhat flexible, but just make sure you focus on your audience to ensure your success.

Getting More Likes and Followers

There is one key secret that you should know about when it comes to social media. The more followers and likes you have will certainly translate into more visitors knowing about the information on your site. Below are some effective methods to develop followers:

Have giveaways and competitions so as many people as possible will be enthusiastic about getting something free. Have a Twitter contest where followers will promote your brand. You will get a great deal of coverage for very little expense.

Make sure to add new content to your site on a regular basis. It’s a good idea to add a few posts every day, for all platforms. Add value to your readers by making posts that are relevant and informative.

Make use of humour (if appropriate) because everyone enjoys a good laugh.

Make the most of current trends in the news that are relevant to your niche. When you do this your site could achieve some level of authority which could increase your page ranking.

Make friends with other authority sites in your niche. If you make friends and also share content with them, they might be more likely to assist you or even exchange resources and information.

Organize Your Campaign

As with all marketing campaigns, it is best to structure it with concise objectives and goals. First, you need to know specifically want you expect from the campaign.

Be Unique

Social media offers your brand a wonderful opportunity to thrive and also acquire a personality. To increase your visitors participation and following, you really do need to be unique.

Use Videos

Online video marketing is a great way to share information and interact with your followers. Have a fun video contest that will engage your followers and keep them interested in your site.

Ways to Structure Your Website

Provide navigation this is user-friendly. Visitors usually give up, and go looking for another site when they find it difficult to navigate a website. You want to keep your visitors users engaged so they will stay on the site.

You have one main goal to accomplish after a user has found your website, is impressed with its style, and interacts with the content. And, that is to keep them on your site longer, because then the odds are in your favour that their visit will result in a sale or lead.

Organize the site’s content so your entry levels contain general information. Make sure to label your content with consistent terminology. Consider using the “3-click rule,” because this makes it really easy to find information. Visitors like it when they only have to make a couple of clicks to find information.

Different Methods for Backing up Your Website

Backing up your website regularly is vital so you don’t lose your important data.

CPanel

One of the quickest and easiest ways to save your data is to use the cPanel administrative interface backup tool. CPanel lets you backup your total site or if you prefer, make partial backups.

WordPress

If you happen to have a WordPress site then you should find backing up easy with their backup plugin. This plugin lets you schedule automatic backups .

Backup Software

Another way to backup your site is to use backup software. There are quite a few like eServerBackup, SiteVault, and Backup Smart. Just remember that really good backup software programs are not free.

FTP Client

FTP (file transfer protocol) is an effective way to not only transfer files, but also a simple way to back up all of your site files. This method may be a little complicated for people that aren’t tech-savvy.

Host Migration

There are times when you may have to change to a new web hosting provider. There are many different migration tools such as Sharegate sharepoint migration tools that can do the task very efficiently. Moving from one host to another is not a complicated process if you remember some important things. The first thing you need to do is backup up your data. Next, give yourself sufficient time to change to your new hosting service. It may cost you more but you could avoid some down time.

Your new host provider will decide which migration tools to use to get the job done quickly. It could take up to three days before your name servers are fully propagated because migration doesn’t happen overnight. Many times when you change web hosting the migration service will be free. Last, but certainly not least, remember to inform your domain name registrar of the move.
