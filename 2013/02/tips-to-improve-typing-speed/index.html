5 Tips to Improve Your Typing Speed

Feb 9th, 2013 | Comments

We are living in a digital age and everything needs computer to get done. Typing has become an essential necessity for people. By being fast with your fingers while typing on the keyboard you are not only going to output more productive work, but also you can make your brain work faster than ever. You may have some better ideas or sentences to frame up in your mind, but it will last just for seconds and you have to use that within the short frame of time. So, typing in a good speed becomes necessary here. This article will help you about certain do’s and don’ts to improve your typing speed.

(Source- Getty Images)

Practice is more important

It needs lots of practice to remember the location of each key in the keyboard. If every time you have to look in the keyboard for searching and entering a key, it will take a lot of time for you to finish even a word. You can allocate some 10 minutes daily to get practiced with the keyboard. You can spend even more time if you are free. But, the important thing is you should not miss out even one day from practice. Once you are practiced to some better extent, then you can take a day break from the routine or you can work on alternative days too. Actually, it won’t take too much time for getting practiced with the keyboard. You can achieve your goal very soon.

Make use of typing test facility

First you need to be aware of how good and fast you are at typing. So, to judge your typing speed, many online testing facilities are there today. Make use of that. Once you are done with the test, you will get the results which will let you know how fast you were typing, what to improve, how much time you took to type the given sentence, what’s your typing speed per word and all such essential details. You can practice typing the Google news sentences or what you read from the local newspaper or anything that you see in front space. With the results, you should be better able to understand where you are lagging and how to improve your issues with that.

Make use of the typing tutorials

(Source- Getty Images)

With so much advantages we are getting from computer and internet, again here comes the one another advantage from internet which is nothing but teaching you about how to type quickly and efficiently. Many online tutorials are there today to help you improve your typing speed. The online tutorials will teach you about perfect hand positioning on the keyboard. It will let you know about when and where you have to use which finger on the keyboard. It will be more helpful for the beginner level people. You will be given some sample text and you have to type the same. You can save your work and take it the next day and so on. This will let you see the progress of your typing ability in a day to day basis.

The typing tutorial software is available for payment too. You can purchase such software and learn from that. You can even try playing games that are specifically designed for testing your typing speed. TypeRacer, sense-lang balloon, typing maniac etc. are some of the examples for such kind of games used for measuring your typing speed.

Be good with the shortcut keys

Almost all of the operations in computer will have shortcut keys that are automatically defined. User friendly shortcut keys are also there where you can define your own shortcut key for some operations based on your convenience and memory power. So, remembering all shortcut keys will increase more productivity and will let you save more time on typing. For example, if you have to save a document, you have to use the mouse to select file and then have to click save or save as and then you should give a file name. Instead of all these steps a simple ctrl+s will do the above same action. The navigation will be very easy with the help of shortcut keys. Similarly, formatting the content of your documents is also an easy task when you use shortcut keys. When you have to do all these tasks within a single document like navigation, formatting, organizing, alignment etc. then the shortcut keys will be more useful in saving more time and your energy.

Here are few more shortcut keys for Windows 8 to improve typing speed:

Ctrl + up/down——— move the cursor to the beginning or end of the current paragraph.

Ctrl + home/end—— Move the cursor to the beginning or end of the page

Ctrl + shift + home/end—– select the content above or below of the current position of the cursor

Ctrl + left/right————move the cursor to one word left or right

Ctrl + shift + left/right—— select the word left or right of the current position of the cursor

Try to avoid seeing the keyboard as possible as you can

(Source- Getty Images)

This is one of the best methods of improving your typing speed. At first, you may be producing so many typos, but the days of practice will fetch the better results. But for this practice, you should be first practiced with the finger placement techniques. You just have to look at the monitor screen of your PC or the sheet you have with the sample sentences. You have to strictly follow the rule of not looking into your keyboard while typing. Just think of a other situation in your life. For example, if you are riding on the road with your car or bike, where will you concentrate? Will you concentrate on the road or on the vehicle? Same logic applies here.

All these tips will be helpful for you to be a better person in typing. The internet has got so much of information about anything and everything and why should not about the improvement of your typing skills. Depend on it and judge your typing speed, practice it, improve it and you are done.
